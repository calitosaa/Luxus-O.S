The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two numbers before it, starting with 0 and 1. The sequence typically goes 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21 and so on, each number getting larger as the sequence progresses. It can be used to find patterns in nature and is popular in mathematics and computer science.
