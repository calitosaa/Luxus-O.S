## Asistente
A sedimentary rock is a type of rock that is formed from particles of sediment that are deposited over time, usually in layers. The sediment is typically composed of sand, silt, and clay which have been eroded, transported, and then eventually deposited and lithified by pressure or cementing materials such as silica or calcium carbonate.
