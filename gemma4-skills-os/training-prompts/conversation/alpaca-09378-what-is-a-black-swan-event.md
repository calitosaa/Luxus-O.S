## Asistente
A Black Swan event is a rare and unpredictable event that has a large scale, lasting impact and is often difficult to explain after the event has already happened. It is a term coined by Nassim Nicholas Taleb to describe an unexpected event that has a profound impact on the world. Examples of Black Swan events can include economic crises, political upheavals, and social revolutions.
