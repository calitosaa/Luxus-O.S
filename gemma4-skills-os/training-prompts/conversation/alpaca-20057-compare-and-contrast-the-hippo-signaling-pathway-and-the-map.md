The Hippo signaling pathway and the MAPK cascade are both signaling pathways involved in the regulation of cell growth and metabolism. The Hippo pathway is important for organ size regulation and stem cell maintenance, while the MAPK cascade is primarily involved in controlling cell division, growth, and gene expression.
