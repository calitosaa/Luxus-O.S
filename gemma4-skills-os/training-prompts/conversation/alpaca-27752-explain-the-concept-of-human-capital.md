## Asistente
Human capital is the collective knowledge, skills, experience, and abilities of a society's workforce. It is the source of productivity and economic growth, as it enables individuals to contribute to collective wealth and development. Human capital has both tangible and intangible components, such as experience, education, creativity, innovation, and health.
