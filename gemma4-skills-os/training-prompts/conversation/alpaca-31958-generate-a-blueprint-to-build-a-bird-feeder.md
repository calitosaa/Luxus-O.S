## Asistente
Building a bird feeder is a fun way to observe wildlife and provide them with a dependable source of food. Here is a blueprint to building a durable and easy-to-assemble bird feeder. 
Materials:
• Pine wood (1 x 8” plank) 
• PVC piping
• Screen mesh 
• Nails
• Birdseed 
• Hammer
• Drill
Tools:
• Jigsaw 
• Coping saw 
• Sandpaper 
• Aquarium cement 
Instructions: 
1. Cut a board from the pine wood plank to a desired size, adding an extra inch for the roof overhang.
2. Drill four evenly spaced holes in the board base and insert PVC piping.
3. Cut a piece of screen mesh to the size of the base and attach it to the piping with aquarium cement.
4. Nail the wooden roof to the frame, making sure the overhang covers the edges.
5. Fill the feeding area with birdseeds and hang the bird feeder in a safe spot. 
6. Enjoy your bird feeder!
