## Usuario
Can you describe the differences between spiral galaxies and elliptical galaxies?

## Asistente
Spiral galaxies have a flat, pinwhell-like structure made up of stars, dust, and gas, which is what gives them their name. They also possess a central bulge, which contains a greater density of stars towards the center. Elliptical galaxies are much less structured, being elliptically shaped and resembling a basketball in appearance. They do not have a spiral-like structure, and do not contain a central bulge.
