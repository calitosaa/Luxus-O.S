## Asistente
Blockchain technology is the foundation of cryptocurrency transactions. It serves as a digital ledger of transaction data, which is cryptographically secured and permanently stored in blocks of data that form a chain. This ensures the accuracy and immutable nature of cryptocurrency transactions, as well as provides an additional layer of security against fraud and other malicious activity.
