Thai Coconut Noodle Stir-Fry 
Ingredients:
- 4 ounces rice noodles
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, julienned
- 2 cups mung bean sprouts
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- 1/2 cup coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons fish sauce
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1/4 cup scallions, chopped
- 2 tablespoons lime juice

Directions:

1. Soak the rice noodles in hot water for 5 minutes, and then drain.
2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Add the garlic, bell pepper, and bean sprouts and cook until the vegetables are just tender.
4. Add the sesame oil, coconut milk, fish sauce, soy sauce, and honey.
5. Simmer the stir-fry for 2-3 minutes and then add the noodles.
6. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the noodles are tender.
7. Turn off the heat and stir in the scallions and lime juice.
8. Serve the Thai Coconut Noodle Stir-Fry hot.
