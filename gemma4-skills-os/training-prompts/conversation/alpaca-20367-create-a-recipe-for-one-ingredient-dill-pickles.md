## Usuario
Create a recipe for one-ingredient dill pickles.

## Asistente
Ingredients:
• 4 cups fresh cucumbers
• 2 tablespoons sea salt
• 1 tablespoon black peppercorns
• 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
• 2 cloves garlic
• 1/2 cup fresh dill
• 2 cups water
• 2 cups white vinegar

Instructions:
1. Scrub the cucumbers and slice them into desired shapes.
2. In a bowl, add the cucumbers, salt, peppercorns, coriander, garlic, and dill.
3. Massage the mixture gently and set aside for 30 minutes.
4. In a medium saucepan, combine the water, vinegar, and remaining spices. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring the liquids occasionally.
5. In a jar or container, layer the cucumber slices and the liquid mixture. Seal the jar or container and refrigerate for at least 3 days before serving.
