## Asistente
Three psychological techniques often used in advertising are positive reinforcement, priming, and repetition. Positive reinforcement encourages people to buy products by rewarding them with discounts, free gifts, or other incentives. Priming involves targeting customers with specific keywords and visuals to evoke a certain emotional response. Repetition is used to make sure that potential customers remember the advertisement and the product associated with it.
