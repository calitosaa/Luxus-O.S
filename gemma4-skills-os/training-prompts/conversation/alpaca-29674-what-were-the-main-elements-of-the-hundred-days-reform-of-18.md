## Asistente
The Hundred Days Reform of 1898 was an ambitious reform program advocated by the Chinese emperor Guangxu for the purpose of modernizing and strengthening the Qing dynasty system in China. The reforms included the establishment of a new educational system, the drafting of a modernized legal code, the reorganization of government departments, the introduction of global merchant trading, the amendment of landholding systems, and the introduction of modern technology to military forces.
