Vegetarian Shepherd's Pie. 
Ingredients: 
- Potatoes 
- Carrots 
- Onions 
- Green peas 
- Vegetable broth 

Instructions: 
1. Preheat the oven to 350 F. 
2. In a pot, boil the potatoes, carrots and onions for about 10 minutes or until the potatoes are soft. 
3. Drain the potatoes and carrots, and mash together in a bowl. 
4. Add the green peas and a cup of vegetable broth and stir until everything is evenly mixed. 
5. Spread the mixture in a baking dish and top with the remaining half cup of vegetable broth. 
6. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. 
Enjoy your flavorful and healthy vegetarian shepherd's pie!
