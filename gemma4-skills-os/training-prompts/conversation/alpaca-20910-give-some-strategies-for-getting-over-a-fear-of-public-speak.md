## Asistente
Here are some strategies you can use to get over your fear of public speaking: 
- Understand the fear: Take time to think through why you’re scared. What’s the source of the fear? Is it the fear of the unknown, or is it something specific? 
- Prepare well: Take time to thoroughly know your material, practice with a friend or in front of a mirror, and be sure to practice out loud. Make sure to practice your delivery, as well as your content.
- Focus on the audience: Spend time thinking about the audience and how you can communicate with them. Talk about something that’s important to them and make sure to provide solutions to their issues.
- Take small steps: Start small and take baby steps, from small gatherings and groups, to larger groups and events. Aim to gradually increase the size of the group over time. 
- Relax and breathe: Remember to take deep breaths and relax before a speech. Focus on your breathing and think positive thoughts. 
- Visualize success: Practice visualizing your success. Picture yourself standing confidently and delivering an engaging speech.

By understanding your fear, preparing well, and taking small steps, you can work towards overcoming your fear of public speaking. With practice and the right strategies, you’ll be a confident public speaker in no time!
