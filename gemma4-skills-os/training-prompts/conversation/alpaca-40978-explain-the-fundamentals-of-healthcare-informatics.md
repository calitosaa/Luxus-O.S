## Asistente
Healthcare informatics is the interdisciplinary study of the design, development, adoption, and application of IT-based innovations in healthcare services delivery, management, and planning. It involves the use of IT systems to collect, store, protect, and analyze data pertaining to healthcare services. It enables the efficient exchange of information among different healthcare providers and patients. It is also used to improve the quality of care, reduce costs, and improve patient satisfaction. Healthcare informatics applies principles from computer science, information technology, and health care, and can involve a broad spectrum of topics from technology-enabled patient care to medical informatics and population health analytics.
