Magnetic force and gravity are both significant forces in the universe. Gravity is an attractive force that acts between any two objects with mass, while magnetic force is the attracting and repelling of magnetic materials and charges. Gravity is relatively weaker than magnetic force, and acts over larger distances in comparison to magnetic force.
