Cheesy Garlic Bread Bites:
Ingredients:
- 1/2 loaf of French bread 
- 2 tablespoons of butter
- 2 cloves of garlic, finely minced
- 1 cup of your favorite shredded cheese
- 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 450F.
2. Cut the French bread into 1-inch cubes and spread them on a sheet pan.
3. In a small bowl, melt the butter and add the garlic, oregano and a pinch of salt and pepper.
4. Pour the butter mixture over the bread cubes, coating evenly.
5. Bake in the oven for 8 minutes or until golden brown.
6. Remove from oven and top with shredded cheese.
7. Place back in the oven for a few minutes until the cheese is melted.
8. Serve warm and enjoy!
