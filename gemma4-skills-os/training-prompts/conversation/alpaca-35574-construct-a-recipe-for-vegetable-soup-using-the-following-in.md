## Asistente
Vegetable Soup Recipe 

Ingredients: 
-1 onion, chopped 
-2 carrots, chopped 
-2 stalks celery, chopped 
-1 zucchini, chopped 
-1 potato, diced 
-1 quart vegetable broth  

Instructions: 
1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pot over medium heat. 
2. Add the onion, carrots, and celery and cook until they are soft and golden, about 5 minutes. 
3. Stir in the zucchini and potato and cook for an additional 5 minutes stirring occasionally. 
4. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring to a low boil. 
5. Reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, until all of the vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes. 
6. Serve hot. Enjoy!
