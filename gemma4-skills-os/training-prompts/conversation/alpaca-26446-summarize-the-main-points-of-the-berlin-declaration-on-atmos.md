The Berlin Declaration on Atmospheric Pollution is a declaration by the Council of Europe that recognizes the importance of protecting the atmosphere from atmospheric pollution. The main points of the declaration include: 
- Recognizing that atmospheric pollution is a fundamental threat to human health;
- Calling upon countries to work together to reduce atmospheric pollution;
- Promoting “cleaner” production processes and technologies;
- Promoting scientific research and education; 
- Improving public awareness of atmospheric pollution; and 
- Enforcing international agreements on atmospheric pollution.
