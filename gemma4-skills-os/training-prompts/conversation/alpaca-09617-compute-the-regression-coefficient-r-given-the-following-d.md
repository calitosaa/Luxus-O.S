## Asistente
The regression coefficient (r) can be calculated using the formula r = (Σxy - (Σx)(Σy)/n)/σxσy, where Σxy is the sum of the products of x and y, (Σx)(Σy)/n is the product of the sums of x and y divided by the sample size, and σxσy is the product of the standard deviations of x and y.
