## Asistente
The present perfect tense is used to talk about actions or events that happened in the past but are still relevant in the present. An example of a sentence in the present perfect tense is: "I have gone to the store many times this week." This sentence explains that the speaker has gone to the store multiple times in the past week, and the action is still relevant to the present.
