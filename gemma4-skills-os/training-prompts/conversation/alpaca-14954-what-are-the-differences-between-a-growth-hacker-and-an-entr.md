## Usuario
What are the differences between a growth hacker and an entrepreneur?

## Asistente
The main difference between a growth hacker and an entrepreneur is the focus on specific strategies to achieve rapid growth. Growth hackers focus on building, testing, and deploying specific tactics that are intended to result in short-term growth, such as acquiring new users, engaging existing ones, or improving customer retention rates. In contrast, entrepreneurs tend to focus more on long-term growth, such as developing new products and services, scaling operations, and strategically expanding into new markets. Both approaches require creative thinking and technical know-how, but growth hackers are often the ones developing, analyzing, and executing the necessary tactics for growth.
