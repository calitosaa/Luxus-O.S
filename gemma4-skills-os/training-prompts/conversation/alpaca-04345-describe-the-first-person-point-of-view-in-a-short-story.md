## Asistente
In a first person narrative, the narrator is a protagonist and the story is told from their perspective. This allows the reader to experience events through the narrator’s eyes and become immersed in the story. It also gives an intimate point of view as the narrator can describe their emotions, thoughts and actions, enabling readers to gain insights about the character’s perspective and opinions.
