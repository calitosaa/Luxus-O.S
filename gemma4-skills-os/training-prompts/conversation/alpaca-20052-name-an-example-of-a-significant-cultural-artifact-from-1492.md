## Asistente
One significant cultural artifact from 1492 is the Zaltrap Map, which was created by cartographer Juan de la Cosa. This map is significant because it is one of the earliest known world maps to feature Europe, Africa, and the Americas on the same page. It is also one of the first expression of the "New World" concept that developed during the Age of Discovery.
