## Asistente
Media literacy is the ability to access, analyze, evaluate, and create media in a variety of forms. It involves becoming aware of the biases, assumptions, and purposes of media messages, understanding the type of information that is presented, and recognizing the persuasive techniques used in various media platforms. By understanding media literacy, individuals can become more critical consumers of information and create more effective media messages.
