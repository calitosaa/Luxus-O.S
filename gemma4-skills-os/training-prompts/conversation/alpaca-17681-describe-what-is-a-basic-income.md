## Asistente
A basic income is a regular sum of money given to an individual or a household by the government irrespective of any other income or employment status. It is intended to provide economic security to those who may not have access to other sources of income, such as paid employment or other benefits. The amount of money provided is typically calculated in relation to the current poverty level in the given region. The basic income is usually provided as a regular sum of money and is not expected to be repaid.
