## Asistente
The four stages of language learning are acquisition, fluency, literacy, and mastery. Acquisition is the initial stage where the learner absorbs and understands the language, fluency is when the learner becomes comfortable speaking and using the language in everyday conversation, literacy is when the learner becomes comfortable reading and comprehending texts, and mastery is when the learner is able to use the language so fluently that it appears native.
