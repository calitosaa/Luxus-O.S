## Usuario
Describe the types of data generated by an e-commerce website.

## Asistente
An e-commerce website can generate various types of data, such as customer data, product data, order data, payment data, delivery data and so on. Customer data includes personal data, such as name and contact information, as well as shopping preferences and behaviour. Product data includes product specifications, pricing, availability, and reviews. Order data includes details of customer orders, such as date and time, payment terms, delivery options, and so on. Payment data includes customer payment details, such as credit card numbers and bank information, as well as payment status. Delivery data includes customer delivery address, and details of when, how, and where an order was delivered to the customer.
