## Asistente
The Turing Test is a method of determining if a computer is capable of exhibiting intelligence similar to that of a human. It is based on a conversation between a human judge and two participants, one of which is a computer program. If the judge cannot identify which one is the computer program, then the machine is accepted as displaying intelligence on a level comparable to a human.
