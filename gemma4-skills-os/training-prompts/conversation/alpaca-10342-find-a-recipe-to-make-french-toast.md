Find a recipe to make French toast.

## Asistente
Ingredients: 
- 4 slices of bread
- 2 large eggs 
- 1/4 cup of milk 
- 2 tablespoons of butter 
- 1/4 teaspoon of salt 
- 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon 
- 1 teaspoon of sugar 

Instructions:
1. Crack the eggs into a shallow bowl and add the milk, salt, and sugar. Beat the mixture until it is blended. 
2. Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
3. Dip the slices of bread in the egg mixture until they are evenly coated. 
4. Place the slices in the skillet and heat for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown. 
5. Flip the slices over and continue to cook until both sides are golden brown. 
6. Transfer the French toast onto a plate and sprinkle with cinnamon. Serve hot.
