## Asistente
Informed consent is a process in which an individual voluntarily agrees to participate in a study or clinical trial after being fully informed about the potential risks and benefits involved. This process is intended to make sure that participants understand what is involved and make a voluntary and informed decision regarding participation. Informed consent is based on the principle that people have the right to make decisions about their own bodies and should have the information they need to make such decisions. It should also clarify whether any compensation is involved and what will happen to the participants’ data.
