## Asistente
Teriyaki Beef Bowl:
Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- 2 green onions, chopped
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook, stirring and breaking up the beef with a spoon until the beef is browned and cooked through.
2. Add the onion, garlic, soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, water, ginger, and sesame oil to the skillet. Simmer for 3-4 minutes until the sauce thickens.
3. Add the green onions and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve over cooked rice for a complete meal.
