Ingredients: 2 eggs, ½ cup chopped peppers and onions, 2 tablespoons olive oil, ¼ teaspoon garlic powder, salt and pepper to taste.

Instructions:

- Add the peppers and onions and cook, stirring often, until vegetables are softened, about 3 minutes. 
- Crack the eggs into the skillet and season with garlic powder, salt and pepper. 
- Stir to combine and cook until eggs are scrambled and cooked through, about 2 minutes.
