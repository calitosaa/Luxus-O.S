## Asistente
Start with a piece of rectangular paper. Fold the paper along its length. Then flip the paper over and fold the edges towards the center. Open the folded paper and fold the top corners of the paper towards the center. Then fold the paper in half vertically. Finally, open up the paper and make a fold along the length of the paper. Turn the paper over and open it. Push up the nose and you have your paper airplane ready to fly.
