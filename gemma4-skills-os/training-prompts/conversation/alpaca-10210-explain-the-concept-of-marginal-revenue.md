## Asistente
Marginal revenue is the additional income a business receives for selling one more unit of its product or service. This is calculated by taking the change in total revenue and dividing it by the change in the quantity of goods or services sold. The marginal revenue of a product or service helps businesses to determine the optimal price point where they can maximize their profits.
