## Asistente
Attention mechanisms are a neural network technique used to identify and focus on important parts of an input. They allow the network to focus on certain input elements while disregarding others, allowing the network to better process and understand the input. Attention mechanisms are typically used in natural language processing tasks, such as machine translation and question answering.
