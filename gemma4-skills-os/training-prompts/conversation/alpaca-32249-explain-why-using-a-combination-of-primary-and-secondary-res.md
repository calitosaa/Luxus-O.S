## Asistente
Using a combination of primary and secondary research is important when conducting market research because it allows you to gain more accurate insights into the target market. Primary research is done through surveys, interviews and focus groups with members of the target audience, which provides a direct understanding of customer preferences. Secondary research reviews existing data such as demographic studies, political trends and economic surveys — it provides additional contextual information that can help inform your primary research efforts.
