## Asistente
Imposter Syndrome is a psychological phenomenon in which people feel a persistent fear of being exposed as a fraud. Despite having high achievements, those affected by this syndrome doubt their abilities and fear that any success they experience is not deserved. Many believe that they will eventually be exposed and do not accept compliments or recognition for their successes.
