## Asistente
Slow fashion is a movement towards more sustainable fashion practices like using long-lasting materials, high quality construction, and ethical practices. Slow fashion is better for the environment, since it encourages reuse, reduces energy consumption, and cuts down on waste. It’s also typically better for people, as it reduces the exploitation of workers and empowers them to create better businesses and better lives.
