## Asistente
Cognitive intelligence includes the ability to use logic, solve problems, and think abstractly. It relies on gaining knowledge through study and practice. In contrast, emotional intelligence focuses on the ability to perceive, understand, and handle the emotions of oneself and others. It involves the ability to recognize, understand and regulate emotions, as well as have empathy, social skills, and self-motivation. Cognitive intelligence is more focused on facts and problems while emotional intelligence is more focused on understanding one’s feelings and the feelings of others.
