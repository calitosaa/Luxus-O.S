Peanut Butter and Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:
- 2 slices of whole wheat bread
- 2 tablespoons of creamy peanut butter
- 2 tablespoons of jelly
- 2 slices of Cheddar cheese
- 2 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
2. Spread peanut butter and jelly on one slice of the bread.
3. Place the Cheddar cheese slices on the other slice of bread.
4. Place the slices of bread with the PB&J and cheese on the skillet, creating a sandwich.
5. Spread butter on the sides of the bread.
6. Toast the sandwich on both sides for about 3 minutes each, or until golden and crispy.
7. Serve the warm and gooey sandwich. Enjoy!
