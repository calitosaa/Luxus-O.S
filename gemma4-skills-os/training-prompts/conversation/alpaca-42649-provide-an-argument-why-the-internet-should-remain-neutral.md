## Asistente
Net neutrality is essential for ensuring an open and competitive internet. Maintaining a neutral internet allows for a level playing field for individuals, organizations and businesses - it enables them to develop, distribute, and access content on their own terms. Conversely, if internet service providers (ISPs) can decide which content is more important or which websites are more advantageous, it would reduce user choice, create an internet where certain companies have an unfair advantage, and limit free expression online.
