## Asistente
Broadcasting is the transmission of a signal through an electrical or optical medium over a wide area. In the case of broadcasting your internet signal, it is the process of sending a data signal from the router to one or more devices in order to establish a connection. This allows multiple devices to access the same connection, enabling multiple people or devices to connect to the same internet service.
