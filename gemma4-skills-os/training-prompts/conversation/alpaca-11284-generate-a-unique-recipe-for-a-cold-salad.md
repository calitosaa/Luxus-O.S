Veggie Quinoa Salad:
Ingredients: 
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 2 cups grape tomatoes, halved
- 1 cup cooked corn
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1/2 cup chopped onion
- 1/4 cup freshly chopped parsley
- 1/4 cup freshly chopped basil
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 2 cloves minced garlic
- Salt and pepper to taste


Instructions: 
1. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions. Let it cool completely.
2. In a large bowl, mix together the cooked quinoa, tomatoes, corn, bell pepper, cucumber, onion, parsley and basil.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic and seasonings.
4. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and stir to combine.
5. Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
