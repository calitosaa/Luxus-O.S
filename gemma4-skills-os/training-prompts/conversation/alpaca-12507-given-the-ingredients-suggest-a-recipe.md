## Asistente
Date, Nut and Honey Energy Bars:

- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
- In a food processor, process the dates, almonds and walnuts until they become a coarsely ground mixture.
- In a microwave-safe bowl, heat the honey and oil until just melted. 
- Pour the honey and oil mixture over the ground date and nut mixture and process until well combined. 
- Grease an 8x8 inch square pan.
- Press the date and nut mixture into the pan, pressing down firmly to ensure that it's packed evenly. 
- Bake in the oven for 15 minutes. 
- Let cool before cutting into bars.
