## Asistente
A bill of lading is a document issued by a carrier to a shipper as a contract of carriage. It is evidence of the details of the shipment, describing the type and quantity of goods being transported, the origin and destination of the goods, the date of shipment, and the parties involved. It also serves as a receipt for goods.
