## Asistente
Instructions for determining the area of a trapezoid:

1. Measure the lengths of the parallel sides of the trapezoid (B1, B2).
2. Calculate the difference between the two parallel sides (B2-B1).
3. Measure the height of the trapezoid (H).
4. Multiply the difference with the height (H x (B2-B1)).
5. Divide the result by two (H x (B2-B1))/2.
6. The result is the area of the trapezoid.
