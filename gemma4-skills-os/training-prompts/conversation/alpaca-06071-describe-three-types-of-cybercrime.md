## Usuario
Describe three types of cybercrime.

## Asistente
Three types of cybercrime are: identity theft, phishing, and ransomware. Identity theft is a type of crime where an attacker steals and uses a person's personal information such as bank accounts, social security numbers, and credit card numbers. Phishing is an attack where a malicious website or email is sent in an attempt to lure the victim into handing over personal information. Finally, ransomware is a type of malicious software designed to encrypt a device and demand payment from the victim in order to gain access to the device.
