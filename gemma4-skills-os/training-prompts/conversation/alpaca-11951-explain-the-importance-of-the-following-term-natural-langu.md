Natural Language Processing (NLP) is an important area of Artificial Intelligence that deals with understanding and analyzing textual data. It enables computers to understand and process large amounts of natural language data, such as text from emails, articles, social media posts and books. NLP also helps machines to understand the intent and sentiment of the text, enabling computers to identify topics, extract key words and phrases, as well as classify text into different categories. Moreover, NLP-based models can also be used to generate new text, such as in chatbots and automated customer service agents.
