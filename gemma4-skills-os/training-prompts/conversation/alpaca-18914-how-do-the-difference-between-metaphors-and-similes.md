## Usuario
How do the difference between metaphors and similes?

## Asistente
A metaphor is an expression used to compare two things without using like or as, while a simile is an expression used to compare two things using like or as. Metaphors provide more direct comparisons, while similes are more specific and compare two ideas in a more detailed way. For example, you might say that someone is "as strong as an ox" as a simile, but you might say that someone is "an ox of strength" as a metaphor.
