## Usuario
Give me a recipe for a delicious peach cobbler.

## Asistente
Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour 
- 2 tablespoons sugar 
- 2 teaspoons baking powder 
- 1/2 teaspoon salt 
- 1/3 cup vegetable oil 
- 2 tablespoons sour cream 
- 1/3 cup of milk 
- 2 cups of peeled, sliced peaches 
- 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar 
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon 
- 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg  

Instructions: 
1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Grease a 9-inch square baking dish. 
2. In a medium bowl mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. 
3. In another bowl whisk together the oil, sour cream, and milk until blended and pour into the dry ingredients. Stir together until just blended, do not overmix. 
4. Place the peaches in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle the brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg over the top. Pour the batter over the peaches. 
5. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until golden brown. Serve the cobbler warm with a scoop of ice cream.
