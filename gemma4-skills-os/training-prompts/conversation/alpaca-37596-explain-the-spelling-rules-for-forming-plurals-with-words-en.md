## Usuario
Explain the spelling rules for forming plurals with words ending with -y.

## Asistente
When a word ends in -y and the letter before the -y is a consonant, the -y should be changed to -i and -es should be added to form the plural. For example, the plural of family is families. However, if the letter before the -y is a vowel, there are usually no changes made to the spelling. For example, the plural of boys is boys.
