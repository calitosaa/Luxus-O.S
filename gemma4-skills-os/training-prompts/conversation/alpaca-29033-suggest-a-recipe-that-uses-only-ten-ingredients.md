Vegetable Fried Rice:
Ingredients:
- 2 Cups cooked rice
- 1/2 Cup frozen carrots
- 1/2 Cup frozen peas
- 1/4 Cup diced red bell pepper
- 1/4 Cup diced onion
- 1/4 Cup diced celery
- 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 Tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons sesame oil
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
Instructions:
1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
2. Add the diced onion, celery, carrots, and bell pepper and cook for about 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
3. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, sesame oil, and garlic powder. Stir to combine. 
4. Add the frozen peas and cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through. 
5. Serve hot.
