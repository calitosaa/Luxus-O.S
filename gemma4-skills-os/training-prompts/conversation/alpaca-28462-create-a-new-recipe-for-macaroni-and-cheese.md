## Asistente
Ingredients:
- ½ lb macaroni
- 2 cups of heavy cream
- 1 ½ cups grated cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup parmesan cheese
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 cloves of minced garlic
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Boil macaroni in salted water until it's al dente. Drain the macaroni, transfer to a large bowl.
3. In a saucepan, heat cream and butter over low-medium heat. Add garlic and stir until butter is melted.
4. Gradually add the cheddar and parmesan cheese, stirring continually until cheese is completely melted and sauce is smooth. Add salt and pepper.
5. Add the cheese sauce to the macaroni and stir until fully combined.
6. Transfer macaroni mixture to a baking dish.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
8. Serve warm. Enjoy!
