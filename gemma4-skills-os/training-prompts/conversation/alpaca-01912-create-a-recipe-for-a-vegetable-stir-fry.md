Vegetable Stir-Fry Recipe: 
Ingredients: 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 1 onion, 2 cloves garlic, 1 bell pepper, 1 carrot, 2 cups mushrooms, 2 cups broccoli, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar, salt and pepper to taste 
Instructions: 
1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
2. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. 
3. Add bell pepper, carrot, mushrooms, and broccoli to the skillet and cook, stirring often, until all vegetables are lightly cooked and tender, about 8-10 minutes. 
4. Add soy sauce and rice wine vinegar to the skillet and stir to combine. 
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve hot.
