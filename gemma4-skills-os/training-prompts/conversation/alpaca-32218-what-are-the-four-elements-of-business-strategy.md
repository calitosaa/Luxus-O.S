## Asistente
The four elements of business strategy are environment analysis, industry analysis, competitor analysis, and strategic planning. Environment analysis looks at the internal and external environment of a business, such as its goals, resources, and culture. Industry analysis looks at the industry in which the business exists, such as its structure, competitors, and customers. Competitor analysis looks at the moves of competitors, as well as their strengths and weaknesses. Lastly, strategic planning plans the groundwork for a strategy to reach the company’s goals.
