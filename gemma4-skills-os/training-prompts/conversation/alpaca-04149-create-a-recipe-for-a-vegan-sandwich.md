## Asistente
Ingredients: 
- 2 slices bread of your choice 
- 2 tablespoons vegan mayonnaise 
- 2 tablespoons vegan pesto 
- 1 sliced avocado 
- ½ cup alfalfa sprouts 
- 1 sliced tomato 
- ¼ cup roasted red pepper 
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder 

Instructions: 
1. Toast the bread according to package instructions. 
2. Spread the mayonnaise and pesto onto the slices of bread. 
3. Top one of the slices with the avocado, alfalfa sprouts, tomato, and red pepper. 
4. Sprinkle garlic powder over the vegetables. 
5. Place the other slice of bread over the vegetables and lightly press. 
6. Cut sandwich in two and enjoy.
