## Asistente
The steps to troubleshoot a laptop battery issue are: 
1. Check if the laptop is plugged into a power source. 
2. Ensure that the AC adapter and power cord are both connected properly.
3. Check the power outlets and reset any circuit breakers if needed.
4. Ensure the battery is not damaged. 
5. Try charging the battery in a different computer to see if the issue is resolved. 
6. Update the laptop’s BIOS to the latest version. 
7. Check if the battery is not visible in BIOS settings.
8. Check battery health in Windows settings or through a battery diagnostics software.
