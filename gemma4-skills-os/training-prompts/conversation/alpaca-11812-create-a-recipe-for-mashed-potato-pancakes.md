## Asistente
Mashed Potato Pancakes
Ingredients:
- 2 large potatoes, peeled and quartered
- 1/4 cup of butter
- 1/4 cup of milk
- 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1/4 cup of grated Cheddar cheese 
- Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
1. Put the potatoes in a pot and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil and cook until tender.
2. Drain the potatoes, then mash them until smooth.
3. Add the butter and milk and mix until combined.
4. Gradually stir in the flour, egg, cheese, salt and pepper until a thick batter forms.
5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and brush with oil. Drop tablespoonfuls of the batter onto the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, flipping once, until golden brown.
6. Serve warm with butter, sour cream and chives. Enjoy!
