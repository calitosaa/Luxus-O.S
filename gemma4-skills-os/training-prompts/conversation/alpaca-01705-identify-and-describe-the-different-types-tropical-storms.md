## Asistente
Tropical storms are powerful and dangerous storms that develop over tropical or subtropical waters. They are classified into different types based on their intensity and size. The most common types of tropical storms are tropical depressions, tropical storms, and cyclones or hurricanes. Tropical depressions are the weakest tropical storms, with maximum sustained winds of no more than 38 mph. Tropical storms have maximum sustained winds of between 39 and 73 mph. Cyclones or Hurricanes are the strongest with maximum sustained winds of 74 mph or more.
