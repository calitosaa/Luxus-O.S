Honey Mustard Bacon Wrapped Shrimp: 
Ingredients: 
- 12-16 medium-large shrimp
- 6 strips bacon
- 1/4 cup honey
- 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. In a small bowl, mix together honey and mustard until fully combined.
3. Peel and devein the shrimp. Wrap each shrimp in a slice of bacon and secure with a toothpick.
4. Arrange the bacon-wrapped shrimp on a baking sheet.
5. Drizzle the honey mustard mixture over the shrimp and then drizzle with olive oil.
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 15-20 minutes until bacon is crisp and shrimp is cooked through.
