Instructions for Making French Toast

1. Gather the ingredients – butter, eggs, cinnamon, sugar, bread slices, and a dash of vanilla extract. 
2. Melt the butter in a shallow bowl and set aside.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, cinnamon, sugar, and vanilla extract.
4. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring it is evenly and completely coated.
5. Heat a skillet on medium heat and lightly grease with some of the melted butter. 
6. Place the soaked bread slices onto the heated skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes until lightly browned. 
7. Flip the slices over and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes on the other side.
8. Serve the French toast warm with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a sprinkle of confectioners sugar. Enjoy!
