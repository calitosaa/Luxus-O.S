## Asistente
Honey Banana Nut Crunch Bowl

Ingredients (Makes 3 servings):
1 cup raw nuts of your choice
2 ripe bananas, mashed
2 tablespoons honey
½ cup blueberries

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. 
2. Place the nuts on a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes or until lightly brown and fragrant. 
3. In a medium bowl, combine the nuts, mashed bananas and honey. 
4. Grease a 9-inch pie dish with butter or cooking spray. 
5. Spread the nut-honey-banana mixture into the pie dish and press down lightly with a spoon.
6. Sprinkle the blueberries evenly over the top. 
7. Bake for 20 minutes or until lightly browned. 
8. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
