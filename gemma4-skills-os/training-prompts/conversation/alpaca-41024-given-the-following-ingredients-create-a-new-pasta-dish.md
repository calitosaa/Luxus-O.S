## Asistente
Creamy Chicken Spinach Pasta

Ingredients (Makes 4 servings): 
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 small onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 cups baby spinach leaves
1/2 cup diced tomatoes
3 cups cooked chicken, diced
1/4 cup cream
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 pound cooked pasta

Instructions:
Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook for 2 minutes. Add spinach, tomatoes, and chicken and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Add cream and cheese and simmer for 5 minutes.
Add cooked pasta to the pan and stir until everything is evenly combined. Cook for an additional 5 minutes or until heated through. Serve warm.
