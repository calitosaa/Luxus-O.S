1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
2. Peel, core, and slice the apples.
3. For the crust, mix the flour, sugar, and salt together in a bowl. Cut in cold butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
4. Gently mix in ice water. Gently fold the dough over onto itself several times. Place in a greased 9-inch pie pan and crimp the edges of the crust.
5. Place the sliced apples into the pastry-lined pie pan. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
6. Cover the apples with a top crust and crimp the edges. Cut a few steam vents in the top crust.
7. Bake for 45 minutes or until the crust is golden.
8. Let cool before serving.
