## Asistente
The primary difference between a standard resume and a CV is length. A resume is typically one or two pages and focuses on relevant skills and experience for the specific job being applied for. A CV is much longer, often five or more pages, and contains more detailed information about an individual's qualifications, research, publications, and awards. Additionally, a CV usually includes a personal profile and references section.
