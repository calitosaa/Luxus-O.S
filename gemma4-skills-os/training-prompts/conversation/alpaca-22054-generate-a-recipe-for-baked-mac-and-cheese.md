## Asistente
Baked Mac and Cheese 
Ingredients: 
-4 cups of macaroni
-4 tablespoons of butter
-4 tablespoons of flour
-4 cups of milk 
-2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese 
-1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese 
-Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions: 
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain it, and set aside.
3. In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk until it forms a paste. Gradually add the milk, whisking continuously until the mixture is smooth. 
4. Continue cooking, stirring often, until the mixture is thick and bubbly. Then add the cheddar cheese and parmesan cheese and stir until the cheese is melted. 
5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
6. In the prepared baking dish, combine macaroni and cheese sauce. Stir until everything is evenly combined. 
7. Bake for 30 minutes, until top is golden and bubbly. Enjoy!
