## Asistente
Apple Fritters: 
Ingredients: 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 2 tablespoons white sugar, 1/4 cup butter, melted, 1 egg, 1 cup milk, 2 cups peeled and diced apples, 1/4 cup of butter for frying, 1/4 cup white sugar for coating. 
Instructions: In a large bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, salt, and 2 tablespoons of sugar. Mix in the melted butter, egg, and milk. Gradually add in the chopped apples until evenly distributed.    
In a large skillet, heat the 1/4 cup butter over medium-high heat. Drop rounded spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil. Fry each side until lightly browned. Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly on a paper towel. Dip the fritters in the 1/4 cup of sugar and enjoy.
