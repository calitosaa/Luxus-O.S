## Asistente
A blockchain is a digital ledger of records, secured using cryptography and managed via a peer-to-peer network of computers. It stores and records transaction data digitally without any need for a central authority or third-party verification. The data stored in a blockchain is immutable and its records are highly secure, meaning they cannot be tampered with or reversed.
