## Asistente
Ingredients:
- 2¼ cups all-purpose flour 
- 1 teaspoon baking soda 
- 1 teaspoon salt 
- 1 cup butter, softened 
- ¾ cup granulated sugar 
- ¾ cup of packed dark brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs 
- 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips 

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Whisk the flour, baking soda and salt in a medium bowl and set aside.
3. In a large bowl, beat the butter and both sugars using an electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy.
4. Add the vanilla, eggs, and mix on low until combine.
5. Gradually add the flour mixture and mix until just combined.
6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
7. Drop dough by 1/4 cupfuls onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.
8. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until golden brown.
9. Cool for 1 minute on the baking sheet before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
