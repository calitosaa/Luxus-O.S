## Asistente
Ingredients:
• 2 bunches fresh spinach, washed and dried
• 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
• 2 cloves of garlic, minced
• 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
• 2 tablespoons honey
• ½ cup muscatel raisins
• Salt and ground black pepper to taste 
Instructions:
1. Put the cleaned spinach in a large bowl.
2. Drizzle the olive oil over the spinach and toss to coat.
3. Add the minced garlic, balsamic vinegar, honey, and muscatel raisins, and season with salt and pepper.
4. Toss everything together and serve.
