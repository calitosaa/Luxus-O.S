## Usuario
Design a recipe for protein waffles.

## Asistente
Ingredients:
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup plain Greek-style yogurt
1/2 cup oat flour
1/4 cup wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3 tablespoons protein powder
2 tablespoons natural honey

Instructions:
1. Preheat waffle iron to medium heat.
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, and yogurt until well blended.
3. In a separate bowl, combine oat flour, wheat flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and protein powder. Add to wet ingredients and mix until combined.
4. Grease the preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Add batter and spread evenly over the surface and close lid.
5. Cook for 4-5 minutes until golden brown.
6.Drizzle with honey and serve warm.
