## Asistente
The four elements of a marketing mix are product, price, place, and promotion. Product refers to the product/service being offered and includes features, quality, design, and brand. Price refers to the pricing strategy (such as list price, discounts, promotions, etc.). Place refers to the channels being used to deliver the product/service to the customer (such as retail stores, ecommerce, etc.).
