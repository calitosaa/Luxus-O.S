## Asistente
Three common manufacturing processes are casting, machining, and injection molding. Casting involves pouring a liquid material, such as liquid metal, into a mold to form a solid object. Machining is a subtractive manufacturing process that includes cutting, drilling, grinding, and other operations to shape a solid material. Injection molding is a process that involves injecting molten material into a mold to produce a formed product.
