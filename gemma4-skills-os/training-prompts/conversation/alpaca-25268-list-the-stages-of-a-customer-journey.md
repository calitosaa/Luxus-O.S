The stages of a customer journey include: awareness, consideration, purchase, loyalty and advocacy. In the awareness stage, customers become aware of a product or service through advertising or other marketing efforts. In the consideration stage, customers begin to research options and weigh their different options. In the purchase stage, customers make their decision and purchase the product or service. In the loyalty stage, customers become repeat customers and start to form an emotional connection with the brand.
