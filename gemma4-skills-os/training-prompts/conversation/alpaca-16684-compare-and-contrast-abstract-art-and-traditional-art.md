Abstract art and traditional art are two different genres of art that have distinct characteristics. Traditional art is typically more representational and realistic, as it involves the accurate representation of a subject matter. Abstract art, on the other hand, is composed of shapes, colors, and forms that may not represent anything in the physical world.
