Leadership focuses on setting an overall direction for an organization, and inspiring and motivating others to follow that direction. Management, on the other hand, is more focused on getting tasks done, and includes activities such as planning, organizing, and controlling. Leadership involves setting a vision, while management involves ensuring that the vision is achieved through conscientious effort.
