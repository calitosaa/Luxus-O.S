## Asistente
Ingredients: 
- 2 large eggs 
- 2 tablespoons of milk
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/4 cup chopped onion
- 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
- 1/4 cup shredded cheese

Instructions: 
1. Beat together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl until fully combined.
2. Heat butter in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat.
3. Add onion and bell pepper to the skillet and cook until tender, about 4-5 minutes. 
4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs are scrambled and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes.
5. Add the shredded cheese and cook for an additional minute until cheese is melted.
6. Serve hot. Enjoy!
