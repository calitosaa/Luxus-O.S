## Asistente
Ingredients:
• 1 package lasagna noodles
• 1 pound ground beef
• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 onion, diced
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
• 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
• 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
• 1 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon pepper
• 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add ground beef, garlic, and onion and cook until beef is evenly browned.
4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper and stir together. Lower heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Spread a layer of sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
6. Spread a layer of meat sauce over the noodles and top with a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat layering process.
7. Bake for 30 minutes. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
