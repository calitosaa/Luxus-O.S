## Asistente
The six sigma process is a set of business strategies that strive to improve the quality of products and services and reduce costs by eliminating errors and defects in the production or service process. It is based on the idea of minimizing or eliminating any variation or differences between expected and actual performance. The six sigma process utilizes data-driven approaches and focuses on identifying opportunities for improvement and optimizing processes.
