## Asistente
1. Appearance: Spiders have eight legs and two body parts while caterpillars have six legs and three body parts.
2. Movement: Spiders move by walking or jumping while caterpillars use their multiple legs to crawl or inchworm. 
3. Diet: Spiders are carnivorous while caterpillars are mostly herbivorous. 
4. Reproduction: Spiders lay eggs while caterpillars undergo a complete metamorphosis, laying eggs that eventually become butterflies or moths.
