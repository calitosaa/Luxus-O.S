## Usuario
Calculate the number of possible outcomes.

Contexto/Input:
A fair coin is flipped 6 times.

## Asistente
The number of possible outcomes is 64. There are 2 possible outcomes for every coin flip (heads or tails), and since 6 coin flips make up one trial, it follows that there are 2^6, or 64, possible outcomes.
