Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Recipe

Ingredients: 
• 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
• 1 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1 teaspoon paprika
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1 zucchini, sliced
• 1 yellow squash, sliced
• 8 cherry tomatoes
• 1 red bell pepper, sliced
• 1/2 onion, diced
• 1 tablespoon Italian seasonings

Instructions:
1. Preheat a grill to medium-high heat.
2. Sprinkle garlic powder and paprika on the chicken breasts and rub with olive oil.
3. Place chicken on the grill and cook for 5–7 minutes per side, or until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
4. Place zucchini, yellow squash, cherry tomatoes, bell pepper, and onion in a large bowl and toss with Italian seasonings.
5. Place vegetables on the grill, spreading them in an even layer. Grill for 5–7 minutes, or until vegetables are lightly charred and tender.
6. Remove chicken and vegetables from the grill and let cool before serving. Enjoy!
