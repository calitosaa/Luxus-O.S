## Asistente
A symmetrical shape is one that has two or more sides that are equal. To identify a symmetrical shape, draw a line through the middle of the shape—if both sides are the same, then the shape is symmetrical. If there are two parts and they are mirror images of each other, then the shape is also symmetrical.
