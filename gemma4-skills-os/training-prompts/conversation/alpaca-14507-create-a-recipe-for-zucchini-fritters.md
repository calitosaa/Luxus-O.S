Create a recipe for zucchini fritters.

## Asistente
Ingredients:
- 2 large zucchinis, grated
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, minced
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 4 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, add the grated zucchini and salt and mix together. Allow to sit for 5 minutes, then squeeze out the excess water with your hands.
2. Add the eggs and all-purpose flour and mix together. Add the minced parsley and black pepper and mix together until everything is well combined.
3. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Drop spoonfuls of the zucchini mixture into the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
5. Serve the zucchini fritters with your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy!
