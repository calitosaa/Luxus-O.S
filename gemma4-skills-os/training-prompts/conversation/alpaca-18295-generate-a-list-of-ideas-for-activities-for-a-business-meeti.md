## Asistente
Here are some ideas for activities for a business meeting:

1. Think Tank – In this activity, the team works together to come up with creative solutions to a specific problem.

2. Problem Solving – Give each small group a different problem and have them work on it for a certain amount of time. Afterwards, have each group present their findings to the rest of the team.

3. Mini-presentations – Have each team member prepare a mini-presentation on a topic that they are passionate about, whether it’s related to their job or something personal.

4. Lucky Draw – Pick names out of a hat, and assign each person to give a short presentation on a surprise topic.

5. Brainstorming Session – Ask each person to come up with one creative idea, and then discuss the ideas together.

6. Role playing – Facilitate a role playing game where each group acts out a particular problem that they are trying to solve.

7. Speed-networking – Set up speed-dating style networking sessions where each person takes a few minutes to state their name, job role, project they are working on and one interesting fact.

8. Scavenger Hunt – Assign each team a list of items to be found in the office or outside. The first team to bring back all the items win!

9. Quiz – Either organize a quiz based on work related topics or assign each team a different topic and have them prepare a trivia game.

10. Movie Analysis – Watch a movie and discuss how the characters, plot and message relates to the current project or issue.

11. Mad Libs – Give each team a worksheet and a fun story template and have them have fill in the words. Share the stories and have a good laugh. 

12. Write a Story – Assign each team a prompt and have them write a story. Afterwards, each team can read and discuss their stories. 

13. Dominoes – Put together a list of thought provoking questions or images and assign numbers to each of them. Have the teams arrange the images/questions/answers like a game of dominoes.

14. Inventions – Give every team a set of random items and challenge them to come up with the most creative invention they can think of.

15. Jigsaw – Cut a puzzle in pieces and each team works on piecing it back together.

16. World Cafe – Have each team come up with a simple question and then the whole group moves around the room and discuss the topic at each table. 

17. Joint Doodle – Give every team a portion of a large paper and have them draw something on it. When joined together, it will become a large masterpiece!

18. Wall Art – Give each team a wall or a canvas and have them decorate it with words, phrases or drawings that represent the team’s project.

19. Yoga – Starting the meeting with a few simple yoga poses and breathing exercises helps to calm nerves, energize the room and set up the meeting in a positive way.
