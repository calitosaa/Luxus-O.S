## Asistente
Apple Crumble Recipe
Ingredients: 
- 4 large apples, peeled, cored and cubed 
- 2 tablespoons of butter 
- ½ cup of packed brown sugar 
- 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour 
- ½ teaspoon of ground cinnamon 
- ¼ teaspoon of ground nutmeg 
- 1 cup of rolled oats 

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch square baking dish.
2. Spread the cubed apples in the greased baking dish. 
3. In a medium bowl, mix together the butter, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and oats until well combined. 
4. Sprinkle the oat crumble mixture over the apples and spread evenly.
5. Bake in preheated oven for about 30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown. Serve warm.
