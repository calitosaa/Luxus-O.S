## Usuario
Describe the fundamental principles of App Design.

## Asistente
The three fundamental principles of app design are accessibility, usability, and user experience. Accessibility focuses on making sure the application is accessible to all users, regardless of ability. Usability focuses on creating an interface that is easy to use, while user experience focuses on creating a pleasing, enjoyable experience for the user. Additionally, app design should consider the user journey, which includes the user’s tasks, goals, and mental processes, in order to create an effective user interface.
