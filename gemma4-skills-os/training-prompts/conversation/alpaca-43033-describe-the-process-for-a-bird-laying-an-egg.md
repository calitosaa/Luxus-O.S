## Asistente
When a bird lays an egg, the process is divided into three parts. Firstly, the egg is formed in the bird's reproductive tract. Then, the egg is covered in a protective coating known as the eggshell which is made from calcium carbonate. Lastly, the egg is laid from the bird's body and enters the nest.
