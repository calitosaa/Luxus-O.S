Veggie Lasagna with Spinach Filling: Preheat oven to 375°F. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat with a few teaspoons of oil. Add 1/2 an onion, diced, and 1/2 a bell pepper, diced, and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes. Add 3 cloves of minced garlic and stir. Add a 10-ounce package of frozen, thawed, and squeezed of excess moisture spinach and cook for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside. In a bowl, mix together 2 cups of ricotta cheese, 1/4 cup of finely grated Parmesan, 1/2 teaspoon of each oregano, basil, garlic powder, and salt, and pepper to taste. Spread 1/2 a cup of store-bought or homemade marinara sauce in the bottom of an 8×8-inch baking dish. Place 4-5 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Evenly spread the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Top with the spinach and vegetable mixture, followed by a layer of noodles. Layer with 1/2 cup of marinara sauce, 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese, more lasagna noodles, the remaining ricotta cheese mixture and vegetables, and 1/2 cup of marinara sauce. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
