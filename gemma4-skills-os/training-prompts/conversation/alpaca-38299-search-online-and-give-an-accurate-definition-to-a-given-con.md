Blockchain is an encrypted digital record of transactions that is organized in a distributed ledger system. The records or "blocks" are linked together using cryptography and stored on public or private networks. Each block contains the transaction data, a timestamp, and a link to the previous block in the chain, thus forming a chain of blocks and creating an immutable ledger of transactions.
