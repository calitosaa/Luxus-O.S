## Asistente
The main difference between an AM radio broadcast and an FM broadcast is the frequency range. AM broadcasts generally have a frequency range between 535 and 1705 kHz, while FM broadcasts range from 88 to 108 MHz. Another difference between the two is the sound quality; FM broadcasts have higher sound quality and less static than AM broadcasts.
