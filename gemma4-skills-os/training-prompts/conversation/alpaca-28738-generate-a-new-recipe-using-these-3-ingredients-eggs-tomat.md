Scrambled Eggs with Tomato and Cheese:

Ingredients:
- 3 eggs
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1/2 cup shredded cheese
- Salt, to taste
- 2 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
1. Heat the butter in a large skillet on medium heat.
2. Add the tomatoes and cook until softened and lightly browned.
3. Crack the eggs into a large bowl and season with salt and pepper.
4. Pour the eggs into the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until lightly scrambled.
5. Add the cheese and stir until melted.
6. Serve warm.
