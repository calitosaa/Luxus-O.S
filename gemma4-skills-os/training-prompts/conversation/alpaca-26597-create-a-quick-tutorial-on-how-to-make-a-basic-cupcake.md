Making cupcakes is an easy and fun project! To make basic cupcakes, you will need the following ingredients:

1. 2 cups of all-purpose flour
2. 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
3. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
4. 1/2 teaspoon salt
5. 1/2 cup of butter
6. 1 cup of white sugar
7. 2 eggs
8. 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

Step 2: In a medium bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir until everything is blended together.

Step 3: In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.

Step 4: Gradually beat in the dry ingredients until everything is mixed thoroughly.

Step 5: Grease a muffin tin with butter. Fill the cups with the batter. 

Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes.

Step 7: Let the cupcakes cool before removing them from the pan. Decorate with your choice of icing and sprinkles!
