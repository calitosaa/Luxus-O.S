## Asistente
Mood and tone are both elements of a piece of writing that can help to convey a certain atmosphere or feeling. However, they are different in that mood is the overall atmosphere that the author is trying to create, while tone is the particular attitude or opinion expressed in the text. Mood is usually expressed more implicitly and often based on a combination of elements, such as setting, character development, and plot. Tone, on the other hand, is expressed more explicitly and often directly attributed to a particular character's dialogue or narration.
