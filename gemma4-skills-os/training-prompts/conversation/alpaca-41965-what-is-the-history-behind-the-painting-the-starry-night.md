## Asistente
The painting ‘The Starry Night’ was painted by Dutch Post-Impressionist painter Vincent van Gogh in 1889. It represents the view from the east-facing window of his asylum room at Saint-Rémy-de-Provence, just before sunrise, with the addition of an idealized village. The painting incorporates the color and movement of the night sky along with its original significance that comes from beliefs of the time. The painting is regarded as one of the most recognized works of art in the world.
