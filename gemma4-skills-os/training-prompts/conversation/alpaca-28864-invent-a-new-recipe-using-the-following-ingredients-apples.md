## Asistente
Apple Onion Garlic Saute:
Ingredients:
- 2 Apples, cored and diced
- 2 Onions, diced
- 4 cloves of Garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons of Olive Oil
- Salt and Pepper, to taste

Instructions:

1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the diced onions and garlic and cook for a few minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened.
3. Add the diced apples and cook for about 3 minutes, stirring occasionally until lightly browned.
4. Add salt and pepper to taste and stir to combine.
5. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until everything is cooked through and apples are slightly caramelized.
6. Serve warm and enjoy!
