## Asistente
Vegan Curry
Ingredients: 
• 2 cans of chickpeas 
• 1 onion, diced
• 1 clove of garlic, finely minced
• 1 inch of ginger, grated
• 1 teaspoon cumin
• 1 teaspoon curry powder
• 1 teaspoon turmeric
Instructions: 
1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add the onion, garlic and ginger. Cook until onions are translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Add the spices and stir until fragrant. 
4. Add the chickpeas and stir to evenly coat with the spice mix. 
5. Add 1 cup of water and bring to a gentle simmer.
6. Cook for 15 minutes or until the sauce has thickened and the chickpeas are soft.
7. Serve over cooked rice, quinoa or cauliflower rice. Enjoy!
