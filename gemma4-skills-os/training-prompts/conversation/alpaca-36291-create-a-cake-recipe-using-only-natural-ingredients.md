## Asistente
Ingredients
- 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour 
- 1 teaspoon baking powder 
- ½ teaspoon baking soda 
- ½ teaspoon salt 
- ½ cup butter 
- 1 ½ cups sugar 
- 2 eggs 
- 2 teaspoon vanilla extract 
- ⅔ cup plain Greek yogurt 
- 1 cup milk 

Directions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F
2. Grease two round cake pans. 
3. In a medium bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt 
4. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until creamy. 
5. Add eggs one at a time, scraping sides of the bowl between additions. 
6. Add vanilla extract and mix. 
7. Add the Greek yogurt and mix until combined.
8. Add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients in three parts, alternating with milk begin and end with the dry ingredients. 
9. Divide the batter between the two cake pans. 
10. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean. 
11. Allow the cakes to cool completely before frosting.
