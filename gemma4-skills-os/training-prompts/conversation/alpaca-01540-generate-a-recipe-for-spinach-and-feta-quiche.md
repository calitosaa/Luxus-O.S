Generate a recipe for spinach and feta quiche.

## Asistente
Ingredients:
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 cups chopped fresh spinach
- 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 4 eggs 
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/2 cup chopped cooked bacon
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 9-inch pre-made pie crust 

Directions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about one minute.
3. Add the spinach and cook until wilted, about 5 minutes.
4. Remove the spinach and garlic from the pan and set aside.
5. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
6. In a large bowl, beat together the eggs, salt, and pepper.
7. Stir in the spinach, feta, bacon, and heavy cream, mixing well.
8. Pour the mixture into the pre-made pie crust.
9. Top with the shredded cheddar cheese.
10. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the quiche is set in the center.
11. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
