## Usuario
How does Newton's second law of motion explain the relationship between force, mass, and acceleration?

## Asistente
Newton's second law of motion states that a net force applied to a mass will cause it to accelerate, and the acceleration is proportional to the force divided by the mass. Therefore, the greater the force applied to an object with a given mass, the greater its acceleration. Conversely, the greater the mass of an object, the less its acceleration will be with a given amount of force.
