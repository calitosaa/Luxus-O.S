## Usuario
Describe the concept of "bag of words" in NLP.

## Asistente
Bag of words is a commonly used term in natural language processing (NLP) when analyzing text data. It is a model that assumes that any given document is a collection or “bag” of words, disregarding the structure and order of the words in the document. Each document is represented as an un-ordered collection of words. Words are weighted in a bag of words model based on the number of times they occur in a document. It is typically used for tasks like document classification and topic modeling.
