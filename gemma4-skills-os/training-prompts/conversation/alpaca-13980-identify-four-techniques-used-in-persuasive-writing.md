## Asistente
Four techniques used in persuasive writing are:
1. Pathos: appealing to the emotions of the reader 
2. Logos: presenting logical arguments and facts 
3. Ethos: establishing credibility to bolster the argument 
4. Inclusive language: using inclusive language to create a sense of unity and agreement
