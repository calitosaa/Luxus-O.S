## Asistente
Bacon and Parsley Stuffed Onions:
Ingredients:
- 4 large onions, peeled and halved
- 4 cloves of garlic, minced
- 4 tablespoons of butter
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 3 tablespoons of chopped parsley
- 8 slices of bacon, cut into small pieces
- Salt and pepper, to taste
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add garlic and onion and cook for 2 to 3 minutes, or until translucent.
4. Remove from heat and let cool for 10 minutes.
5. In a medium bowl, combine chopped parsley, bacon, and onion mixture.
6. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
7. Fill each onion half with the bacon and parsley mixture.
8. Place onions onto a baking dish and bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
9. Serve and enjoy!
