## Asistente
Wind erosion is a process by which the wind transports sediment from one location to another. This can have a devastating effect on soil as it can be stripped away and deposited elsewhere. The wind can also expose new areas of soil to wind and water erosion, which can exacerbate the damage. In addition, wind erosion can cause increased sedimentation in rivers and other water bodies, which can lead to further environmental degradation.
