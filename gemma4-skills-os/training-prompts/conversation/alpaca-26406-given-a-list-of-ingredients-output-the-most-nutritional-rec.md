## Usuario

Contexto/Input:
Chicken breast, brown rice, broccoli, carrots, onions

## Asistente
One-Pot Chicken and Rice with Vegetables. 
Ingredients: 
- 2 chicken breasts, cubed
- 1 cup brown rice
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1/2 cup thinly sliced carrots
- 1/2 cup diced onions
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the chicken cubes and cook until the chicken is cooked through, stirring often.
3. Add the brown rice, broccoli, carrots and onions to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are softened, stirring often.
4. Season the mixture with garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper. Stir to combine.
5. Reduce the heat to low and cover the skillet. Cook until the chicken and rice are cooked through, stirring occasionally.
6. Serve. Enjoy!
