## Asistente
Article 1 of the US Constitution establishes the legislative branch of government. It establishes Congress, a bicameral legislature composed of the House of Representatives and the Senate. It outlines the rules, powers, and duties of each chamber and provides a process to create and pass laws. Article 1 also gives Congress the power to levy and collect taxes, borrow money, regulate commerce, and raise and maintain the armed forces.
