## Usuario
Design an engaging lesson plan on the topic “The Five Major Religions”.

## Asistente
Objective:  By the end of the lesson, students will understand the five major religions, their traditions, and their places of origin. 

Activity 1: Brainstorming (5 minutes) 
The teacher will lead a discussion about the five major religions: Christianity, Islam, Buddhism, Hinduism, and Judaism.

Activity 2: Worksheet (15 minutes)
Students will complete a worksheet that has questions about the five major religions. 

Activity 3: Group Work (15 minutes) 
The teacher will divide the class into small groups and assign each group a religion. The group will have to research the religion, answer questions, and present their findings to the rest of the class.

Activity 4: Discussion (15 minutes) 
The teacher will lead a discussion and ask the following questions: What similarities do the five major religions have? What are the differences between the five major religions? 

Activity 5: Quiz (15 minutes) 
Students will take a quiz on the five major religions to assess their learning.
