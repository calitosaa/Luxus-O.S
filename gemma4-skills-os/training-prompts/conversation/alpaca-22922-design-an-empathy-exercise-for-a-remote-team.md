## Asistente
One exercise for a remote team to foster empathy is the “However, I understand” exercise. Teams would be divided into pairs and give each other a situation that causes the other person to feel frustrated. The listener would then summarize their understanding of the situation and respond with “However, I understand why you feel that way,” without trying to offer any solutions. This allows everyone to recognize and validate each others’ feelings and be more understanding and empathetic with their teammates.
