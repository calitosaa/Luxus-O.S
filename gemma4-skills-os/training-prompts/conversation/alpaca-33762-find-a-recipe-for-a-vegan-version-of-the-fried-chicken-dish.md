## Usuario
Find a recipe for a vegan version of the fried chicken dish.

## Asistente
Ingredients: 
- 2 cups all-purpose flour 
- 2 tablespoons paprika 
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder 
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme 
- 1 teaspoon black pepper 
- 3/4 teaspoon salt 
- 1 teaspoon onion powder 
- 2 cups unsweetened almond milk 
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar 
- 2 tablespoons olive oil 
- 1/2 teaspoon ground sage
- 1 cup vegan bread crumbs 
- 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast 
- 4-5 medium sized potatoes, cut into cubes 
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder 
- 2 tablespoons vegan butter 
- Salt and pepper, to taste 
- 5 cloves garlic

Instructions: 
1. In a bowl, mix the flour, paprika, garlic powder, thyme, black pepper, salt, and onion powder.
2. Add in the almond milk, apple cider vinegar, olive oil, and ground sage. Mix together until a thick batter is formed. 
3. In a separate bowl, mix together the vegan bread crumbs, nutritional yeast, and chili powder. 
4. Dip each potato cube into the batter and then coat them in the vegan bread crumb mixture. 
5. Heat vegan butter in a pan over medium-high heat. Once heated, cook the potatoes for about 4 minutes on each side. 
6. Add the garlic before flipping each side of the potatoes to ensure the garlic doesn’t burn. 
7. Once the potatoes are golden and crispy, remove from heat. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. 
8. Serve hot and enjoy!
