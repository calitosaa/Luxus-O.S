## Asistente
The idiom "A Taste of Your Own Medicine" refers to a situation where a person experiences the same thing they have subjected someone else to. It is used to suggest that a person must experience what they have done to another in order to understand the consequences of their actions. It implies that the person must feel what they have inflicted upon someone else in order to learn a lesson.
