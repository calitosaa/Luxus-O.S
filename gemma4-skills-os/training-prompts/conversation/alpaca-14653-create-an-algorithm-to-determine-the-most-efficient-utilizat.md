## Asistente
Our proposed algorithm for determining the most efficient utilization of a virtual assistant will analyze the tasks that the assistant can perform and the types of requests it receives. It will then assign tasks to the assistant based on the time it takes to complete each task and the associated urgency for completion. The algorithm will also take into account the importance of each task and the needs and expectations of the customer. Furthermore, it will consider factors such as the availability of the assistant, potential conflicts between tasks, and potential issues that can arise from the task being performed wrong or being incomplete. Through this algorithm, we can ensure that each task is given to the assistant in the most efficient manner and that each customer’s needs are met.
