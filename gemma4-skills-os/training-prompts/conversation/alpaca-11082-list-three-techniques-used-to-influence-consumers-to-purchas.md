## Usuario

## Asistente
Three techniques used to influence consumers to purchase products are:
1. Social proof: Using endorsements from friends, family, or social media influencers to create a perception of popular demand. 
2. Loss aversion: Making consumers believe they will miss out on something if they do not buy the product. 
3. Scarcity: Creating a sense of urgency by using limited offers or limited quantities of a product.
