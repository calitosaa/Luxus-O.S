## Asistente
The different types of conflicts include interpersonal conflicts, intrapersonal conflicts, performace conflicts, resource conflicts, role conflicts, values conflicts, and task conflicts. Interpersonal conflicts arise when individuals have conflicting goals, wants, or needs. Intrapersonal conflicts occur when individuals are in conflict with themselves. Performance conflicts occur when individuals have difficulty completing tasks or achieving goals. Resource conflicts arise when individuals cannot access or effectively manage the resources they need to complete their tasks. Role conflicts occur when individuals have competing roles or responsibilities that are in conflict with each other. Values conflicts occur when an individual's beliefs are not in alignment with others. Finally, task conflicts occur when individuals do not agree on how to approach a task or project.
