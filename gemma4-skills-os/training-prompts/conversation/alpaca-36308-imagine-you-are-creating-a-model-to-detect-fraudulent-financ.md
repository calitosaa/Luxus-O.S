Imagine you are creating a model to detect fraudulent financial transactions. What metrics are important to measure when evaluating this model?

## Asistente
When evaluating a model for detecting fraudulent financial transactions, it’s important to measure metrics such as accuracy, recall, precision, F1 score, and the false positive rate (FPR). Accuracy measures the overall performance of the model, while recall and precision measure how effective the model is at identifying true positives and true negatives. F1 score measures the balance between precision and recall, and FPR measures the percentage of false positives relative to true positives.
