## Usuario
Create a lesson plan that teaches students how to use a ruler.

## Asistente
Objective: Students will learn how to use a ruler. 

Materials:
- 1 ruler per student
- Blank paper 

Activity:
1. Explain the purpose of a ruler (e.g., measuring length, drawing straight lines). 
2. Show students how to read the measurements on a ruler by identifying the major numbers (e.g., 0, 1, 2...) and fractions of an inch. 
3. Guide the students to practice taking measurements from the origin to various points listed in a worksheet. 
4. Ask the students to practice measuring objects in the classroom and drawing horizontal/vertical lines using a ruler 

Evaluation: 
- Ask students to take measurements of various objects around the classroom and record their answers in their notebooks. 
- Ask students to draw horizontal and vertical lines on blank paper.
