Quick Lasagna
- 2 cups of cooked lasagna noodles 
- 1/2 cup ricotta cheese 
- 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese 
- 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese 
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil 
- 1 small onion, diced 
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced 
- 2 (26 oz) jars of spaghetti sauce 
- 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano 
- 1/4 teaspoon of dried thyme 

In a small skillet, heat the olive oil over low-medium heat. Sautée the onion and garlic for 3 minutes. Add the spaghetti sauce and herbs and simmer for 10 minutes. In an 8x8 inch baking dish, layer half of the cooked lasagna noodles. Top with half of the ricotta cheese and half of the mozzarella cheese. Spread half of the sauce over the cheese. Place the remaining lasagna noodles over the sauce and top with the remaining ricotta and mozzarella cheese. Pour the remaining sauce over the cheese and sprinkle with the parmesan cheese. Bake at 350 degrees F for 15 minutes.
