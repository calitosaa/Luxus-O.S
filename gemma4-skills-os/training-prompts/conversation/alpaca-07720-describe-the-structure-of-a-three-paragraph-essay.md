## Asistente
A three paragraph essay usually consists of an introductory paragraph, a body paragraph, and a concluding paragraph. 
The introductory paragraph introduces the topic and provides a brief overview of the main points discussed in the essay. 
 The body paragraph is the main portion of the essay, and usually consists of several paragraphs providing evidence and arguments to support the main point.
The concluding paragraph draws together the evidence presented in the body and restates the main points of the essay. It should also provide a call to action or pose a question to the reader.
