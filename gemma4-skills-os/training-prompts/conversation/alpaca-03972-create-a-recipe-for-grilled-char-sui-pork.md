## Usuario
Create a recipe for grilled char sui pork.

## Asistente
Ingredients:
- 1 lb. char sui pork
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:
1. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium-high heat.
2. Rub olive oil all over the pork.
3. In a small bowl, mix smoked paprika, garlic powder, cumin powder, salt, and black pepper together; then rub onto the pork.
4. Transfer the pork onto the preheated grill.
5. Cook for 5-7 minutes on each side.
6. While the pork is cooking, mix honey with a splash of water in a small bowl.
7. Once both sides of the pork are fully cooked, brush the honey mixture over the pork.
8. Serve with your favorite side dish.
