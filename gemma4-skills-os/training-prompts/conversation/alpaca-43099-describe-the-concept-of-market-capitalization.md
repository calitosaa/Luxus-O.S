## Asistente
Market capitalization, also known as market cap, is a measure of the total value of a publicly traded company. It is calculated by multiplying the total number of a company's outstanding shares by its current stock price. Market cap is an important metric used to value a company and can give investors an idea of how big a company is and its potential for growth.
