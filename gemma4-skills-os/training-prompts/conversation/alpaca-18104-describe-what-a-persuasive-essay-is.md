## Asistente
A persuasive essay is an essay that utilizes logical reasoning, facts, examples and other forms of argument to convince readers of a certain point of view. It is a form of argumentative writing that aims to persuade the reader to accept the writer's point of view and take a particular action. The purpose of a persuasive essay is to convince the reader to agree with the author's position on a particular topic. It is important to remember that a persuasive essay should be based on evidence and facts, rather than personal feelings or beliefs.
