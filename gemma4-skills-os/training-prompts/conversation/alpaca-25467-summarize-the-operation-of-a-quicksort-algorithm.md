## Asistente
Quicksort is a divide-and-conquer algorithm that recursively sorts a list of elements. It begins by selecting a pivot element, which divides the list into two partitions, then recursively sorts the sub-partitions by repeating the process until the list is fully sorted. Quicksort is efficient in practice and can be most easily implemented with an in-place partitioning algorithm, so it has good space complexity.
