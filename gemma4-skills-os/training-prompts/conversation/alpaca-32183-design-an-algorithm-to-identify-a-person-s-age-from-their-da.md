## Asistente
Step 1: Get the date of birth from the user.

Step 2: Calculate the current year.

Step 3: Calculate the difference between the current year and the year of birth.

Step 4: Add 1 to the difference if the current month is lower than the month of birth.

Step 5: If the current day is lower than the day of birth, then subtract 1 from the difference.
