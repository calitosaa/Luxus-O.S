## Usuario
Create a recipe for cherry cheesecake.

## Asistente
Ingredients:
- 1 ½ cups of graham cracker crumbs
- 4 tablespoons of butter, melted
- 16 ounces of cream cheese, softened
- 2/3 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
- 3 cups of fresh pitted cherries 
- ¼ teaspoon of salt

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C) and grease an 8 or 9 inch springform pan.

2. In a medium bowl, mix together the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture onto the bottom and up the sides of the pan.

3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.

4. Pour the cream cheese mixture evenly into the graham cracker crust.

5. Scatter the cherries over the top of the cheesecake.

6. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes. Let cool before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
