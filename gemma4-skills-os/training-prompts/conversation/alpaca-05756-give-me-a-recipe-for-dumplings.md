Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup water
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 ½ cups chopped vegetables of your choice 

Directions:
1. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the water and oil. Pour into the dry ingredients and mix with a spoon until a smooth dough forms.
3. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and roll out to 1/8 of an inch thick. Cut out circles with a 3-inch biscuit cutter.
4. Place a tablespoon of the chopped vegetables in the center of each dough circle, then fold the edges together to enclose the filling, creating a half-moon shape.
5. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Carefully slide the dumplings into the water and simmer for 5 minutes. 
6. Serve dumplings with your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy!
