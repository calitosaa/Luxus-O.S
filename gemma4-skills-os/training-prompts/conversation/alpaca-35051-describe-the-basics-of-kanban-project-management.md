## Asistente
Kanban is a type of project management methodology which focuses on visualizing the workflow and streamlining processes to improve productivity. It is based on the fundamental principle that tasks should not be started until an existing task is finished. The core components of Kanban project management include visualization of the workflow, setting of work-in-progress limits, breaking down of tasks into manageable pieces, and focusing on continuous improvement. Kanban also encourages regular team communication and collaboration among members.
