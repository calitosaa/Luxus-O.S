## Asistente
An imperative sentence is a statement that conveys a command or request. It usually takes the form of an order, such as “Close the door.” Meanwhile, a declarative sentence is a statement that conveys information and generally follows a complete sentence structure with a subject and verb. It typically expresses facts, opinions, or beliefs, such as “The door is closed.”
