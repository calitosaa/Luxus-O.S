The primary causes of environmental pollution in the United States are air pollution, water pollution, and land pollution. Air pollution is caused by sources such as burning fossil fuels, industrial processes, and transportation. Water pollution is caused by sewage and industrial waste, runoff from farms and lawns, and improper disposal of hazardous materials.
