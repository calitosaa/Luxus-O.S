## Asistente
A data analyst is responsible for gathering, cleaning, and organizing data from various sources in order to identify trends and insights. They examine the data for patterns and correlations, and generate insights that can be used for decision-making. They create reports and dashboards to communicate results and work with various stakeholders to identify areas for improvement. They also develop predictive models to anticipate trends and outcomes. Data analysts are also often responsible for designing and optimizing data systems, as well as auditing data sources to ensure accuracy.
