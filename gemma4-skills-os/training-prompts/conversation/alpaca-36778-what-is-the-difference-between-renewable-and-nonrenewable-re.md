## Asistente
Renewable resources are those which can be replenished relatively quickly through natural processes. Examples include solar, wind and geothermal energy, as well as plant and animal products such as wood and wool. 

Nonrenewable resources are those which take a much longer time to be replenished and are limited in supply.
