## Asistente
A good formal essay should include a clear introduction that identifies the main idea of the essay and the thesis statement; body paragraphs that discuss the evidence and arguments in support of the thesis statement; a conclusion that summarizes the evidence and arguments presented and restates the thesis statement; and a well-constructed bibliography of sources used in the essay.
