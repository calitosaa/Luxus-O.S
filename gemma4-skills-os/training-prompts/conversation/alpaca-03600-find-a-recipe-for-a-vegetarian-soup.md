## Asistente
This recipe for Vegetarian Soup is a delicious and healthy way to enjoy plant-based meals. You will need the following ingredients: 
- Olive oil 
- 1 yellow onion, diced 
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced 
- 1 red pepper, diced 
- 1 inch piece of ginger, grated 
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder 
- 1 teaspoon cumin 
- 1 teaspoon paprika 
- 1 teaspoon celery seed 
- 4 cups vegetable broth 
- 2 cups cooked cannellini beans 
- 2 cups cooked chickpeas 
- 1 cup cooked quinoa 
- 1 cup diced tomatoes 
- 1 cup diced zucchini 
- Juice of 1/2 lemon 
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 

Instructions: 
1. In a large soup pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the onion, garlic, and red pepper, and cook for about 5 minutes, until softened. 
2. Add the ginger, garlic powder, cumin, paprika, and celery seed, and cook for 1 minute more.
3. Pour in the vegetable broth, beans, chickpeas, quinoa, tomatoes, and zucchini. Simmer for 15 minutes until the vegetables are tender. 
4. Add the lemon juice and parsley. Serve hot with garlic bread, if desired.
