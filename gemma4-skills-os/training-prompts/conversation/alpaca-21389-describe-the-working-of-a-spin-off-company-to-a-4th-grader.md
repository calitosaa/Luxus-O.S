## Asistente
A spin-off company is like a new company that starts from something that already exists. It starts because the company, or part of it, has a different idea or goal than the original company. So the spin-off company goes off on its own and becomes its own thing. For example, a toy company might have a section that makes dolls, so they could make a spin-off company that only makes dolls. That way the dolls have their own company and do things the way they want to.
