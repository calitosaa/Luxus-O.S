## Asistente
Positive reinforcement is a powerful tool for coaches to use when working with a team. It involves praising and rewarding the athletes for their efforts, whether big or small. This can be done by making a point to recognize when something is done correctly, offering words of encouragement, or providing small incentives like a team snack or reward if they reach a certain goal. This can help foster a sense of achievement and motivation among the players, and sends a clear message that their coach believes in them and is committed to helping them reach their goals.
