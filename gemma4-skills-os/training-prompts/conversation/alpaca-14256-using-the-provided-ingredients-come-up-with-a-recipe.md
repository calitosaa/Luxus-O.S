## Asistente
Roasted Carrot, Celery and Onion Soup

Ingredients:
- 3 cups chopped carrots
- 2 cups chopped celery
- 1 cup chopped onions
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon dried thyme
- 4 cups vegetable stock
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
2. In a large bowl, combine the carrots, celery, onions, olive oil, garlic powder and thyme.
3. Spread the mixture onto a baking sheet or dish and bake for 25 minutes, or until the vegetables are just tender.
4. Place the roasted vegetables into a large pot and add the vegetable stock.
5. Simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
7. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
8. Serve warm.
