## Asistente
The three main types of bilingualism are simultaneous bilingualism, sequential bilingualism, and concurrent bilingualism. 

Simultaneous bilingualism occurs when two languages are learned simultaneously from birth. 

Sequential bilingualism occurs when one language is learned first and a second language is then acquired later in life. 

Concurrent bilingualism occurs when two languages are learned at the same time in grade school without the need for one language to be learned first.
