## Asistente
Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:
- 2 slices of white bread 
- 2 slices of cheddar cheese 
- 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:
1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
3. Place one slice of bread on the skillet, butter-side down.
4. Layer the other slice of bread with the cheese, then place on top of the first slice of bread with the butter-side up.
5. Grill for about 2 to 3 minutes, until the bread becomes golden brown.
6. Flip the sandwich and continue grilling for an additional 2 minutes until the cheese is melted and the sandwich is cooked through.
7. Remove from heat and serve. Enjoy!
