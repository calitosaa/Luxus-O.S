The key difference between democracy and autocracy is that in a democracy power is held by the people and their elected representatives, while in an autocracy power is held by a single individual or group of individuals with absolute control. Democracies are governed by various forms of law, including national laws and international laws, while autocracies do not adhere to any law and have no predetermined limits of power.
