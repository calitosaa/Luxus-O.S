## Asistente
The challenges of deep learning include the following:

1. Data processing: the need to process large amounts of data in order to accurately train deep learning models.
2. Model capacity: limited computing resources that can lead to models with limited model capacity.
3. Interpretability: deep models may produce results that are hard to interpret and explain.
4. Overfitting: deep learning models may succumb to overfitting due to large datasets and complex structures.
5. Computational costs: deep learning models require extensive computational resources that are often costly.
6. Limited generalization ability: while deep models can achieve impressive results on specific tasks, they may lack the generalization ability to adapt to new tasks.
