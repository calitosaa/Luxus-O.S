## Asistente
Veggie Stir-Fry 
 
Ingredients: 
• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 
• 1 onion, chopped 
• 1 bell pepper, chopped
• 2 cloves garlic, minced 
• 1 cup mushrooms, sliced 
• 1 cup carrots, chopped 
• 1 cup snow peas 
• 2 cups broccoli florets 
• 2 tablespoons soy sauce 
• 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce 
• 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
 
Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. 
2. Add onion and bell pepper; cook until softened, about 5 minutes. 
3. Add garlic and mushrooms; cook for 2 minutes. 
4. Add carrots, snow peas, and broccoli; cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
5. Add soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame seeds; cook for 2 minutes. 
6. Serve over rice or noodles.
