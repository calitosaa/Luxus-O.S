## Asistente
Two non-alcoholic drinks that contain lemon juice and ginger are a ginger lemonade, and a lemon and ginger spritzer. 

Ginger Lemonade:

Ingredients:
- 6 cups of water 
- 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons of grated ginger
- 2 tablespoons of honey
- 3 tablespoons of sugar

Instructions:

Step 1: Heat the water in a large saucepan over medium heat 
Step 2: Stir in the lemon juice, ginger, honey, and sugar and bring to a boil.
Step 3: Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
Step 4: Strain the mixture through a sieve and serve the ginger lemonade over ice.

Lemon and Ginger Spritzer:

Ingredients:
- 4 ounces of club soda 
- 2 ounces of lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
- 1 teaspoon of sugar

Instructions:
Step 1: In a tall glass, combine the club soda, lemon juice, grated ginger, and sugar. 
Step 2: Stir until all of the ingredients are dissolved.
Step 3: Add a few ice cubes to the glass.
Step 4: Garnish and serve.
