## Asistente
Roasted Carrots and Potatoes with Thyme:
Ingredients:
- 4-5 Carrots, chopped
- 2-3 Potatoes, chopped
- 2 tablespoons of thyme
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Optional: 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese
Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. In a bowl, mix the chopped carrots and potatoes with the thyme and oil until they are evenly coated.
3. Spread the coated vegetables on a greased baking sheet and season with salt and pepper.
4. Roast in the oven for 35-40 minutes, stirring once halfway through.
5. Sprinkle with grated parmesan cheese, if using, and enjoy!
