The American Revolution was a period of conflict between the British colonial powers and the emerging United States of America. It began with the Stamp Act of 1765, which levied taxes on the colonists, and increased calls for independence from Britain. These calls were galvanized by the Declaration of Independence on July 4th, 1776.
