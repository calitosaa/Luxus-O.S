Virtual reality (VR) is an immersive experience that simulates physical presence in a virtual environment, by combining reality and virtual elements together such as sight, sound and touch. It typically involves a headset, such as an Oculus Rift, that users wear to access and interact with 3D computer-generated worlds– users can look around and interact with objects as if they were there.
