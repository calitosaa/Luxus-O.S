## Asistente
Ingredients: 
• 4 medium potatoes
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 teaspoon salt 
• 1 teaspoon chili powder 

Instructions: 
1. Preheat oven to 400°F. 
2. Cut potatoes into 1/8-inch thick slices. 
3. Place potatoes in a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and chili powder. 
4. Spread the potatoes evenly on a baking sheet. 
5. Bake for 20 minutes, then turn the chips over and bake for an additional 20 minutes until crispy. 
6. Serve and enjoy!
