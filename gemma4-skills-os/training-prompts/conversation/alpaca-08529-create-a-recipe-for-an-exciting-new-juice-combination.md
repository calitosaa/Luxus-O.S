Blackberry, Ginger and Orange Juice

Ingredients:
- 2 cups of fresh blackberries
- 1 large ginger root, peeled and grated 
- 2 oranges, peeled and juiced

Instructions:
1. Place the blackberries, ginger and orange juice in a blender and process until smooth. 
2. Pour the blended juice through a strainer to remove the seeds and ginger chunks.
3. Serve the juice in a glass over ice. Enjoy!
