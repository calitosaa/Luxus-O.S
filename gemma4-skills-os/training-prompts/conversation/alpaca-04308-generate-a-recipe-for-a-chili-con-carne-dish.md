## Asistente
Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 can red kidney beans
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- Optional toppings: sour cream, shredded cheese, avocado

Instructions:
1. In a large pot over medium heat, cook the ground beef until no longer pink, approximately 5 minutes.
2. Add the chopped onion and bell pepper, and cook for 3 minutes. Then, add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, red kidney beans, cumin, oregano, and chili powder, and bring to a simmer.
4. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook for 15 minutes.
5. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper, if needed.
6. Serve chili con carne with optional toppings, such as sour cream, shredded cheese, and avocado. Enjoy!
