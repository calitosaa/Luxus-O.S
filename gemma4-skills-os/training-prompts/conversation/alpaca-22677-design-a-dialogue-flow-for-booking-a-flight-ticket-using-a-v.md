## Usuario
Design a dialogue flow for booking a flight ticket using a virtual assistant.

## Asistente
User:  Hi, I would like to book a flight ticket. 
Assistant: Where would you like to travel? 
User: I want to go to San Francisco.
Assistant: What date do you plan to travel?
User: May 15th.
Assistant: Are you looking for a round-trip or one-way ticket?
User: Round-trip.
Assistant: Any preference for time of travel?
User: Yes, I would like a flight in the morning.
Assistant: What time would you like to depart? 
User: 9 AM.
Assistant: Do you have any preferences for airline, class, or price? 
User: Yeah, I would like a cheap ticket on a good airline.
Assistant: Okay, I'm checking the available flights to San Francisco on May 15th. Here are the results... (provides flight results) Would you like to book any of these? 
User: Yeah, I'll go with the 11AM flight on Delta. 
Assistant: Okay, I have booked the flight for you. Your ticket is now confirmed!
