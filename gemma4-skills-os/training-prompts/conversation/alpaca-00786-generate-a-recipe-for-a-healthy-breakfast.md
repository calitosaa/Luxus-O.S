Chocolate Protein Pancakes:
Ingredients:
- 1 cup oat flour
- 2 scoops chocolate protein powder
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 2 ripe bananas
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup almond milk
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons of honey
- 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
Instructions:
1. In a mixer, blend together oat flour, protein powder and baking powder.
2. In a separate bowl, mash the bananas and whisk in the eggs.
3. Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until well combined.
4. Then add the almond milk, cinnamon, honey and coconut oil.
5. Heat a pan over medium heat and grease with cooking spray.
6. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan and cook for about 2 minutes per side.
7. Serve with fresh fruit and honey. Enjoy!
