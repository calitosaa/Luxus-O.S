## Asistente
Novice writers often make the following common mistakes:

1. Not proofreading the text and correcting errors.
2. Not having a clear structure and an organized narrative.
3. Not providing enough examples and supporting evidence for claims made.
4. Not providing enough background information on a topic.
5. Not using transitions to move from one idea to another.
6. Not using active voice when possible.
7. Writing in a dull and verbose way, rather than choosing vigorous and concise words.
8. Overusing adverbs and adjectives, instead of relying on powerful nouns and verbs.
9. Not paying enough attention to the aesthetics of the text, such as font choice and spacing.
10. Not revising the text to make it easier to read, such as cutting unnecessary words.
