Chocolate chip cookies: 

Ingredients: 1 cup of flour, 1/2 cup of sugar, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup of butter
Instructions: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. In a large bowl, mix together the flour and sugar.  In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and butter. Slowly add the egg and butter mixture to the flour and sugar, stirring until all the ingredients are fully combined.
