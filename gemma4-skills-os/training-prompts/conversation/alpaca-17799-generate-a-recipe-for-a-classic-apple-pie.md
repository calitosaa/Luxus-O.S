Generate a recipe for a classic apple pie.

## Asistente
Ingredients:  4 apples (diced), 2 tablespoons of lemon juice or white vinegar, 1/2 cup of sugar, 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour, 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg, 1 tablespoon of butter, 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust. 

Instructions: 
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
2. In a large bowl, combine the apples, lemon juice, sugar, flour, cinnamon, and nutmeg and mix until evenly blended.
3. Spread the butter over the bottom of the pie crust.
4. Pour the apple filling into the crust.
5. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
6. Reduce heat to 375°F and bake for an additional 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
7. Allow to cool before serving. Enjoy!
