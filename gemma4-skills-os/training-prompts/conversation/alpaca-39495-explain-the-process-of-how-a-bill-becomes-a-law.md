## Asistente
The process of how a bill becomes a law is complex and involves multiple steps. The process typically begins when a bill is introduced in the House of Representatives or the Senate. If the bill gets a majority vote and passes in both chambers, it is then sent to the president. If the president signs the bill, it is officially law.
