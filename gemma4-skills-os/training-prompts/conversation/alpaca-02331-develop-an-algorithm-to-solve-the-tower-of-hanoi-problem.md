## Asistente
An algorithm to solve the Tower of Hanoi problem can be expressed as follows:

1. Move the n disks from the starting tower to the auxiliary tower, one at a time, using the final tower as the spare

2. Move the nth disk from the auxiliary tower to the final tower

3. Move the n-1 disks from the auxiliary to the final tower, one at a time, using the starting tower as the spare

4. Repeat steps 1-3 until all disks are moved from the starting tower to the final tower
