This delicious one pot egg fried rice is a quick and easy meal with only 5 ingredients! It's a kid friendly recipe full of flavor that you can make in just 20 minutes. 

Ingredients: 
- 2 cups cooked white rice
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- Salt to taste 

Instructions:
1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, then add the cooked rice and season with a pinch of salt.
2. Stir and cook for 2 minutes.
3. In a bowl, beat the eggs together with the milk and a pinch of salt.
4. Pour the egg mixture into the rice and stir to combine.
5. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are set and the rice is cooked through. 
6. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
