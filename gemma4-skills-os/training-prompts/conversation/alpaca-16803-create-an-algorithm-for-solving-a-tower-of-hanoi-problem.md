## Asistente
A Tower of Hanoi algorithm consists of three steps. 
1. Move the largest disk to the other peg. 
2. Move the remaining disks to the other peg in a legal order. 
3. Move the largest disk back to its starting peg. 

To complete the algorithm, you must first define how to move a disk and how to identify when a move is legal. You can then use the three steps mentioned above to complete the algorithm.
