Here are some tips on how to become a better public speaker:

1. Practice: Make sure you practice your speech or presentation well ahead of time, to ensure that you know it inside out.
2. Research: Research the topic you’re speaking about so that you have a broad understanding of the topic and are informed on the current issues surrounding it.
3. Body Language: Make sure your body language is engaging and inviting. Make eye contact with the audience and move around the room to engage them more.
4. Stay Focused: Monitor your thoughts and stay focused on what you are trying to convey.
5. Timing: Be aware of how much time you are taking and make sure to keep to it.
6. Connect: Find ways to relate to the audience and be open and honest in order to make a connection with them.
7. Breathe: Not only to calm down before you start, also to help you find your way back on track if you lose your way.
8. Visuals: Utilize visuals effectively to help engage the audience more in your message and make it easier to remember.
