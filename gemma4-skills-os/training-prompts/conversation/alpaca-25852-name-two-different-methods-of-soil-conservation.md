## Usuario
Name two different methods of soil conservation

## Asistente
Two methods of soil conservation are crop rotation and conservation tillage. Crop rotation involves planting different kinds of crops in a certain order to prevent soil degradation. Conservation tillage is a form of tillage that preserves the existing soil structure by leaving crop residue and covering the soil surface with it. Other forms of soil conservation include contour farming, terrace farming, and cover crops.
