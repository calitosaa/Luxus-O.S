## Asistente
A virtual assistant (VA) is a software that performs tasks on request such as scheduling meetings, creating documents, researching information, and more. VAs typically combine machine-driven automation with human guidance, performing both routine and complex tasks. The aim is to free up a user’s time so they can focus on more important work. VAs can be used in both the personal and professional space, making them a versatile and powerful tool.
