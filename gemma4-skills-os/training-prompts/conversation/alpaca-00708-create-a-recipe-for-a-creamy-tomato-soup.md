Create a recipe for a Creamy Tomato Soup.

## Asistente
Ingredients: 1 onion, diced; 2 cloves garlic, minced; 2 tablespoons olive oil; 28 ounces canned crushed tomatoes; 2 cups vegetable or chicken stock; 1 teaspoon dried oregano; 1 teaspoon dried basil; 1/2 teaspoon sugar; 1/2 teaspoon salt; 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper; 1/2 cup heavy cream. 

Instructions: 
1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until soft, about 3 minutes. 
2. Add the crushed tomatoes, stock, oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes. 
3. Remove from heat and stir in the cream. 
4. Puree with an immersion blender or in two batches in a blender until smooth. 
5. Return to pot and bring to a simmer if necessary. Serve hot.
