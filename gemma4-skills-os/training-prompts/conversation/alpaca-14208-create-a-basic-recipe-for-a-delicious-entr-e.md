## Asistente
Vegetable pasta with olive tapenade:
Ingredients:
• 1 pound whole wheat spaghetti
• 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
• 1/4 cup capers
• 2 minced garlic cloves
• 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
• 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
• 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
• Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
1. Cook the spaghetti al dente according to package instructions.
2. Meanwhile, blend the olives, capers and garlic in a food processor.
3. Heat the olive oil in a skillet. Add the olive mixture and sauté for 2 minutes.
4. Add the parsley, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper, then stir.
5. Drain the spaghetti, reserve 1/2 cup of cooking liquid, and add the spaghetti to the skillet.
6. Cook for 1 minute, stirring, and add some of the reserved cooking liquid if necessary until desired consistency.
7. Serve with extra parsley and red pepper flakes, if desired.
