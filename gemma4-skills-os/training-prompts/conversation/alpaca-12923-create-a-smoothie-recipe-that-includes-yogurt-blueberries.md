## Asistente

• ½ cup frozen blueberries
• 2 tablespoons peanut butter
• ½ cup milk
• 1 banana
• 2 tablespoons honey
• 2 tablespoons flaxseed

Directions:

1. Combine the yogurt, blueberries, peanut butter, milk, and banana in a blender. Blend until smooth.
2. Add the honey and flaxseed and blend for an additional 30 seconds.
