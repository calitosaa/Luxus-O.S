## Usuario
Construct a recipe for banana pancakes

## Asistente
Ingredients: 
- 2 cups of all-purpose flour 
- 1 teaspoon of baking powder 
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt 
- 2 large eggs 
- 2 ripe bananas 
- 1/3 cup of brown sugar 
- 2 tablespoons of butter 
- 2 cups of whole milk

Instructions:
1. Preheat a non-stick pan over medium-high heat.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
3. In a separate small bowl, combine the eggs, bananas, brown sugar, and butter.
4. Gently stir in the milk and the banana mixture into the flour mixture.
5. Spoon 2 tablespoons of batter onto the preheated pan and cook until lightly golden.
6. Flip and cook the opposite side.
7. Serve with syrup or whipped cream and enjoy!
