## Asistente
One of the best ways to prevent overfitting in a deep learning model is to use regularization techniques. Regularization techniques add constraints to the model, such as limiting the size of the weights and the number of layers used. This limits the complexity of the model and prevents it from learning on the noise in the data. Other techniques such as early stopping, data augmentation, and dropout can also be used to prevent overfitting.
