## Asistente
Political landscapes refer to the complexity of issues that political actors have to navigate when looking to achieve objectives. This can include the structure of the political environment, relationships between parties, and levels of public support. The concept also includes recognizing the changes in the political environment and how to use them to one’s advantage. It requires an understanding of the existing political context and being able to predict and use the effects of changes in the political landscape.
