## Asistente
Vegetarian Sandwich:

Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar 
- 1 clove of garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons of balsamic vinaigrette
- 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
- 1/4 teaspoon of salt 
- 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
- 2 slices of mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup of black olives
- 1/4 cup of red onion, diced 
- 1/4 cup of bell peppers, diced
- 1/4 cup of spinach, washed

Directions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. 
2. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. 
3. Add the tomato paste, vinegar, and balsamic vinaigrette, stirring to mix. 
4. Add the salt and pepper and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
5. Remove from heat and set aside.
6. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
7. Spread both slices of bread with the tomato mixture.
8. Layer the mozzarella cheese, black olives, onion, bell peppers, and spinach in the center of the bread.
9. Bake for 10 minutes until the cheese is melted and the bread is lightly browned. 
10. Slice and enjoy!
