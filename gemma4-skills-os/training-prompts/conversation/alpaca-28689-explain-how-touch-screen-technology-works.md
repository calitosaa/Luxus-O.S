## Asistente
Touch screen technology works by detecting the electrical properties of contact through the user’s body. When the user touches a certain part of the touch screen device, it detects an electrical difference of contact. The device then translates this difference into a figure or coordinates and then performs the function the user has requested, such as clicking a button or entering characters.
