## Asistente
Vegan Taco Recipe

Ingredients: 
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 6 soft corn tortillas

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, mix together the beans, peppers, garlic, olive oil, chili powder, cumin and salt and pepper.
3. Spread the mixture on the prepared baking sheet and bake in the oven for 25 minutes.
4. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
5. To assemble the tacos, layer one teaspoon of the filling onto each corn tortilla.
6. Serve warm. Enjoy!
