## Asistente
The steps for changing the oil in a car are:
- Locate the oil drain plug and oil filter, and ensure that the engine is off. 
- Jack up the car and secure it on jack stands. 
- Place a drain pan beneath the oil drain plug. 
- Loosen the oil drain plug and let the old oil drain into the pan.
- Replace the oil filter and oil drain plug and tighten. 
- Fill the engine with the correct amount and grade of oil. 
- Lower the car off the jack stands and start the engine to check for any leaks.
