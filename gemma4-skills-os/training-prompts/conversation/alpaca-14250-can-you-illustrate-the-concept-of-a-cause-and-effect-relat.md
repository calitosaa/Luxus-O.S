## Asistente
A cause and effect relationship is when one event causes another event to happen. This relationship is often a simple one-on-one relationship, where an action from one element causes a reaction from another. An example of this relationship could be a car parked in direct sunlight on a hot day, causing the car's interior to become hot. The cause was the car being parked in direct sunlight, and the effect was the car's interior becoming hot. This relationship can also be seen on a larger scale, such as when deforestation causes global warming.
