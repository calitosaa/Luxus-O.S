## Asistente
Tree rings are important because they can offer valuable information about the environment and climate conditions in which a tree has grown over time. By studying tree rings, we can better understand things like drought, temperature, insect infestations, and even how daily weather has affected tree growth. Tree rings also provide a valuable record of past land use, as well as a way to date the trees they come from, which can be important when working to restore damaged ecosystems. Additionally, tree rings offer valuable insight into climate changes over time and can help us better understand how these changes impact the environment, and how to better prepare for them in the future.
