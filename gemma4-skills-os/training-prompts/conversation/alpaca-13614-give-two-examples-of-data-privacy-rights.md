## Asistente
Two examples of data privacy rights are the right to access one’s personal data and the right to data portability. The right to access personal data gives individuals the ability to request, receive, and review the data that a company has collected on them. The right to data portability gives individuals the right to request that their data be transferred to another data controller. Both of these rights are part of the European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation.
