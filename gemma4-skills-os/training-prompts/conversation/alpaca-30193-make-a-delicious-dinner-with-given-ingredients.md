Marinated Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry 
Ingredients: 
- 2 chicken breasts, cut into small cubes 
- 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce 
- 2 tablespoons honey 
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder 
- 2 tablespoons olive oil 
- 1 red pepper, sliced 
- 1 cup mushrooms, sliced 
- 2 cups kale, roughly chopped 
Instructions: 
1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, honey, and garlic powder. 
2. Add chicken cubes to the bowl and stir to coat. Allow chicken to marinate for 15 minutes. 
3. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat olive oil. 
4. Add chicken cubes and cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
5. Add mushrooms and red pepper slices, and cook for an additional 5 minutes. 
6. Add kale and continue to cook 2-3 minutes, or until veggies are tender. 
7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
