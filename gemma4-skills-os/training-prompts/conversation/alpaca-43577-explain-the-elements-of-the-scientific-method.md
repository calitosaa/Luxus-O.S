## Asistente
The scientific method consists of six elements: making observations, forming a hypothesis, conducting experiments, analyzing and interpreting data, drawing a conclusion, and developing a theory. Making observations involves observing and recording events and phenomena, while forming a hypothesis involves proposing an explanation for the phenomenon being studied.
