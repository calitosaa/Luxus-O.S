## Asistente
Regular expressions are a sequence of characters used to match a pattern in a string. They are used to validate and extract information from a string, while wildcards are special characters that represent multiple characters. Wildcards are used in commands and programs to match a group of related characters and can be used to access files and directories in the Operating System.
