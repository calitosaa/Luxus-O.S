## Asistente
One type of layer used in neural networks is the fully connected layer. This layer is composed of neurons that are connected to every neuron in the preceding layer, and pass their output values to every neuron in the succeeding layer. This layer performs the bulk of the operations in a neural network, such as identifying patterns and making decisions.
