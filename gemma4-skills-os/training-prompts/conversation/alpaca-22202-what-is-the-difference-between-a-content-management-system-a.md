## Usuario
What is the difference between a content management system and an enterprise resource planning system?

## Asistente
A content management system (CMS) is an application used to create, manage and store digital content, such as webpages and blog posts. It is designed to allow multiple users to access and manage content with limited technical knowledge. An enterprise resource planning (ERP) system is a suite of enterprise-level applications used to guide and support a variety of business operations, such as finance and accounting, inventory, and customer relationship management. ERP systems are designed to streamline processes and optimize operations, while a CMS is designed to manage digital content.
