## Asistente
AI (Artificial Intelligence) is the broader concept of machines being able to carry out tasks in a way that is similar to the way people do. It includes Machine Learning, where machines can learn from data to make predictions or take suitable decisions to complete a given task. ML (Machine Learning) focuses on the development of computer programs that can access data and use it to learn for themselves without being explicitly programmed.
