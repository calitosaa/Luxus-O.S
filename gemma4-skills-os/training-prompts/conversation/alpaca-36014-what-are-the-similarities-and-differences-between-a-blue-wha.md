The blue whale and sperm whale differ mainly in terms of size, shape, and diet. The blue whale is the largest animal on Earth and can reach lengths of up to 33 meters and weigh up to 200 tons. On the other hand, the sperm whale is much smaller and can reach lengths of up to 18 meters and weigh up to 50 tons.
