## Asistente
A Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack is a malicious attempt to disrupt the normal traffic of a server, network, or website. It is done by flooding the target with more traffic than it can handle, in an effort to overwhelm the system and make it unavailable to legitimate users. The attack usually originates from multiple sources, hence the term distributed.
