## Asistente
Bean and cheese tacos
Ingredients:
• 1 can black beans
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 small onion, chopped
• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon chili powder
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
• Salt and freshly ground black pepper
• 8 small corn or flour tortillas
• ¼ cup cilantro, chopped
• ¼ cup crumbled queso fresco
• ½ cup shredded cheese

Directions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 2 minutes.
2. Add the beans, chili powder, cumin, cayenne pepper, and salt and pepper to taste. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes.
3. Warm the tortillas in a hot skillet or in the microwave. Divide the bean mixture among the tortillas, top with cilantro, cheese, and queso fresco.
4. Serve the tacos with salsa or other toppings of your choice. Enjoy!
