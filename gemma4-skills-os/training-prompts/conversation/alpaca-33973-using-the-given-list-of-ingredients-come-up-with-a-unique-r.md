Balsamic Honey Tomato Garlic Pasta -
Ingredients: 
- 4-6 tomatoes (cut into wedges)
- 5 cloves of garlic (minced)
- 1/4 cup of balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 cup of honey
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt
- Pepper to taste
- 1 pound of pasta
Instructions: 
- In a large pan, heat the tomatoes, garlic, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt and pepper.
- Cook for about 5 minutes and reduce the mixture until it thickens.
- In a separate pot, cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package.
- Once cooked, drain the pasta and add it to the tomato mixture.
- Heat for for an additional 3-4 minutes.
- Serve with parmesan cheese and fresh basil. Enjoy!
