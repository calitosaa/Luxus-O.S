## Asistente
The five main perspectives of psychology are behaviorism, cognitive psychology, psychodynamic, humanistic, and evolutionary. Behaviorism is focused on the external behavior of organisms while cognitive psychology is focused on internal mental processes. Psychodynamic looks at the unconcious motivations of behavior. Humanistic is focused on individual potential and growth. Finally, evolutionary psychology looks at behavior in the context of natural selection.
