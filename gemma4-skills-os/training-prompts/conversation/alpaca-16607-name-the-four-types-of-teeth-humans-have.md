## Usuario
Name the four types of teeth humans have.

## Asistente
Humans have four types of teeth: incisors, canines, premolars, and molars. Incisors are located at the front of the mouth and are used for cutting and biting food. Canines are the sharp teeth next to the incisors and are used for tearing food. Premolars are behind the canines and are used for crushing and tearing. Molars are the flat teeth in the back of the mouth and are used for grinding food.
