## Asistente
Vegan Creamy Baked Mac and Cheese
Ingredients: 
- 1/2 pound elbow macaroni
- 2 tablespoons vegan butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 cups unsweetened soy or almond milk
- 1 cup vegan cheese
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions: 
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
3. In a saucepan over medium-low heat, melt the butter. Add the flour, and whisk until bubbly.
4. Gradually whisk in the soy or almond milk and bring to a boil. Continue to boil and whisk for 1 minute until thickened.
5. Remove from the heat and stir in the cheese, salt, garlic powder and pepper.
6. Stir the cheese sauce into the cooked macaroni.
7. Transfer the macaroni and cheese to a lightly greased baking dish.
8. Bake in preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the top is lightly browned.
9. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
