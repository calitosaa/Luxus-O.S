Blockchain and artificial intelligence (AI) are both relatively new technologies that are transforming the way we live and do business. Blockchain is an immutable digital ledger technology that uses cryptographic techniques to securely store data in a distributed, decentralized manner, while AI is an area of computer science that uses powerful algorithms and techniques to enable machines to learn, understand and reason like humans.
