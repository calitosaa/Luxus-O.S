Create a recipe for making a simple but tasty burger dip.

## Asistente
Ingredients:
- 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1 can (10 oz) diced tomatoes with green chilies, drained
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup sliced scallions
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
- 1/4 cup sliced pickled jalapeños (optional)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, tomatoes, cheddar, scallions, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until combined.
3. Spread the mixture evenly in a 9-inch pie plate. Top with the pickled jalapeños, if using.
4. Bake for 25 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
5. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving. Serve with chips, crackers, or veggies.
