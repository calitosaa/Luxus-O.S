## Asistente
A tic-tac-toe game is a two-player game where each player takes turns and marks one of nine spaces on a three-by-three grid. The player who gets three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) is the winner. If all nine spaces are filled and neither player has three in a row, the game is a draw.
