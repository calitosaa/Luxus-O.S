## Usuario
Create a lesson plan to teach a 6th grader the basics of strategic planning.

## Asistente
Objective: Students will gain a basic understanding of strategic planning

Materials: Posters of strategic planning definitions and examples

Time: 40 minutes

Lesson:

1. Introduce the concept of strategic planning (15 minutes):
  - Explain the concept of strategic planning and why it is important
  - Provide examples and discuss the benefits of strategic planning
  - Show posters of strategic planning definitions and examples 

2. Break students into groups to analyze and discuss sample strategic plans (10 minutes)

3. Debrief each group about their sample plan (15 minutes):
  - Ask each group to discuss their sample plan and what they learned
  - Guide the students to identify the strengths, weaknesses and key takeaways from each plan
  - Ask questions and facilitate discussion to ensure that the students were able to identify and understand the elements of the plan
 
4. Conclude the lesson (5 minutes):
  - Have students summarize what they learned about strategic planning 
  - Clarify any misunderstandings and answer any remaining questions
