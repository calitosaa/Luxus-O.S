Overnight Oats with Raspberries and Walnuts:

Ingredients: 
- 1 cup of old-fashioned rolled oats
- 1/2 cup of unsweetened almond milk
- 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- 1/2 cup of plain Greek yogurt
- 1/4 cup of walnuts
- 1/2 cup of fresh or frozen raspberries 

Instructions:
1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the rolled oats, almond milk and cinnamon. Stir until combined.
2. Cover the bowl and place it in the refrigerator overnight.
3. In the morning, remove the bowl from the refrigerator and stir in the yogurt, walnuts and raspberries.
4. Divide the mixture into two bowls and enjoy!
