## Asistente
The main function of a refrigerator is to keep food and beverages cold and fresh by maintaining a temperature below the ambient temperature. It does this using a refrigeration cycle powered by a motor compressor. The compressor draws in refrigerant vapor and compresses it, which raises the temperature and pressure.
