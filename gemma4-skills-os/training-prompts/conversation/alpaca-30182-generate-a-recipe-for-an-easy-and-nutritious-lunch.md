Veggie Quinoa Bowl: 

Ingredients:
- 1 cup quinoa 
- 2 cups vegetable broth 
- 1 cup diced carrots 
- 1 cup diced celery 
- 1 cup frozen corn 
- 1 can diced tomatoes 
- 1 onion, diced 
- 2 cloves garlic, minced 
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: 
1. In a saucepan, bring vegetable broth to a boil. 
2. Add quinoa and reduce heat, cooking for about 15 minutes. 
3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add carrots, celery and frozen corn. 
4. Saute for about 5 minutes. 
5. Add diced tomatoes, onion and garlic, cooking for about 5 minutes more. 
6. Add vegetables to the quinoa and season with salt and pepper. Serve warm.
