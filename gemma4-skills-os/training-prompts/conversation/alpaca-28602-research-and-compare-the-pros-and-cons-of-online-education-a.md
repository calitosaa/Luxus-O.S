## Asistente
Online education has several advantages compared to traditional classroom education. It is more convenient as it allows students to learn at any time and place, as long as they have a device and a connection to the internet. This also eliminates the need for physical classrooms and reduces costs for students. Furthermore, students have access to a wealth of resources such as videos, quizzes and other interactive elements. However, one of the biggest disadvantages of online education is that it removes the personal interactions between teachers and students. In traditional classroom settings, students are able to interact directly which can help them to understand concepts better. Additionally, they may be able to ask questions which can't be done in an online environment.
