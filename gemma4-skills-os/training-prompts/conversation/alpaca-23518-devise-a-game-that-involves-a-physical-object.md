## Asistente
Fruit Roll:
Players involved: 2 or more
Object: 8 Fruit objects (suggested: 4 apples and 4 oranges)
Objective: Be the first player to stack all 8 fruit objects in descending order of size - largest at the bottom and smallest at the top.

Gameplay:
1.Players take turns rolling a die
2.The player who rolled the highest number starts off with one fruit object 
3.On their turn, the player rolls the die and moves the fruit object to the next player clockwise
4.The player who is receiving the fruit object must attempt to add this object to the stack
5.If successful, they have earned the right to roll the die - starting the sequence again
6.The first player to successfully stack all 8 fruit objects in descending order is the winner!
