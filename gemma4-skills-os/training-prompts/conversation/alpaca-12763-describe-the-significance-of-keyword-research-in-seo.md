## Asistente
Keyword research is an essential step in any search engine optimization (SEO) strategy. By conducting keyword research, businesses can gain insight into the types of words and phrases that their target audience is commonly using when performing a search. This information can then be used to optimize the website for those keywords, as well as to create website content and marketing campaigns that will be more likely to be seen by the target audience.
