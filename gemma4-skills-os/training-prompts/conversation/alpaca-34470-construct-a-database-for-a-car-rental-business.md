## Asistente
A database for a car rental business can include tables for customers, rental vehicles, rental locations, rental services, rental contracts, and payments. The customer table should store customer information like name, address, payment history, and contact information. The rental vehicle table should store information like make, model, year, and availability. The rental location table should store the address, capacity, and amenities of rental locations. The rental services table should store services like car washes, oil changes, and extras. The rental contracts table should store information like the pick-up and drop-off dates, times, and location, as well as the duration and cost of the rental. Finally, the payments table should store data on payments received and owed.
